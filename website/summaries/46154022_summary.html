State of AI: An Empirical 100T Token Study with OpenRouter

The article is a comprehensive overview of the State of AI in 2024, focusing on the OpenRouter platform's observations and data analysis. It covers several key areas including model usage, pricing trends, performance benchmarks, and emerging trends in the AI landscape.

The report begins by detailing the most popular models used on OpenRouter, noting a shift in preference towards more cost-effective and efficient models. It highlights the rise of models like Llama 3, particularly the 8B parameter version, as a strong contender due to its performance-to-cost ratio. The report also addresses the continued popularity of established models like GPT-4, especially for tasks requiring high levels of reasoning and accuracy, despite their higher cost.

Pricing is a significant theme throughout the article. It discusses the dynamic nature of AI model pricing, emphasizing the trend toward lower costs per token. This decrease is attributed to increased competition, improved hardware efficiency, and advancements in model architecture. The report also points out the variances in pricing across different models and providers, advising users to carefully consider their needs and budget when selecting a model.

Performance benchmarks are another critical component of the analysis. The article presents data on the speed and accuracy of various models, offering insights into their strengths and weaknesses. It acknowledges that while some models excel in certain areas, others offer a better balance between performance and cost. The report encourages users to conduct their own benchmarks to determine the best model for their specific use case.

Emerging trends discussed in the article include the growing importance of fine-tuning and customization. It notes that many users are now fine-tuning open-source models to optimize them for specific tasks or domains, resulting in improved performance and reduced costs. The report also touches on the increasing adoption of multimodal models capable of processing various types of data, such as images and audio, alongside text.

Furthermore, the article delves into the topic of AI safety and responsible development. It highlights the importance of addressing potential risks associated with AI, such as bias and misuse. The report also mentions efforts to develop techniques for improving the transparency and interpretability of AI models, making them more trustworthy and accountable.

Finally, the report provides insights into the future of AI, predicting continued advancements in model performance, efficiency, and accessibility. It anticipates that AI will become increasingly integrated into various aspects of our lives, transforming industries and creating new opportunities. The article concludes by emphasizing the importance of staying informed about the latest developments in AI and engaging in responsible innovation.
