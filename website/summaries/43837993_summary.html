Only Teslas exempt from new auto tariffs thanks to 85% domestic content rule

The article discusses how Tesla is the only car manufacturer that is exempt from new auto tariffs, thanks to the 85% domestic content rule. The new tariffs are aimed at encouraging manufacturers to source more parts domestically and reduce reliance on foreign suppliers. Tesla qualifies for the exemption because their vehicles meet the 85% domestic content threshold.

Key Points:

  • Tesla is the only automaker exempt from new auto tariffs.
  • The exemption is due to Tesla meeting the 85% domestic content rule.
  • The new tariffs aim to encourage domestic sourcing of auto parts.
