AMD continues to chip away at Intel's x86 market share

According to Mercury Research's latest data, AMD has continued to increase its market share in the x86 CPU market. The report indicates that AMD now holds over 25% of the overall x86 market share. Furthermore, AMD powers 33% of all desktop systems. This signifies a continued erosion of Intel's dominance in the CPU market, with AMD steadily gaining ground across various segments.

Key Points:

  • AMD's overall x86 market share now exceeds 25%.
  • AMD powers 33% of all desktop systems.
  • AMD is continuing to gain market share from Intel in the x86 CPU market.
