A message from Intel CEO Lip-Bu Tan to all company employees

Summary:

In a public letter, Intel CEO Pat Gelsinger addresses Intel employees, customers, partners, and shareholders, outlining his commitment to the company and its future. Gelsinger emphasizes a renewed focus on Intel's core mission: to create world-changing technology that enriches the lives of every person on Earth. He highlights the significant progress Intel has made in its transformation journey, driven by its IDM (Integrated Device Manufacturing) 2.0 strategy. This strategy combines internal manufacturing, external foundry partnerships, and in-house design capabilities to deliver leadership products. Gelsinger points to advancements in process technology, architecture, and packaging, all contributing to Intel's roadmap for regaining technology leadership. He underscores the importance of a strong culture centered around innovation, execution, and customer obsession. The CEO acknowledges the challenges ahead but expresses confidence in Intel's ability to overcome them, driven by its talented workforce and its commitment to delivering value to its stakeholders. He reinforces Intel's role in the global technology landscape and its dedication to driving innovation that benefits society.

Key Points:

  • Pat Gelsinger reaffirms his commitment to Intel's success.
  • Intel is focused on creating world-changing technology.
  • IDM 2.0 strategy is central to Intel's transformation.
  • Progress in process technology, architecture, and packaging is highlighted.
  • Intel aims to regain technology leadership.
  • A strong culture of innovation, execution, and customer obsession is crucial.
  • Intel is dedicated to delivering value to stakeholders.
  • Intel plays a vital role in the global technology landscape.
