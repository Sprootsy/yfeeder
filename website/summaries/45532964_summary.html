US Launches Financial Rescue of Argentina, Treasury Buys Pesos

According to a statement by Michael Bessent, a managing partner at Bessent Capital, a $20 billion US-Argentina currency swap has been finalized. The arrangement, which has been under discussion, is now set for implementation. The article highlights the completion of this significant financial agreement between the US and Argentina, as confirmed by a principal figure at Bessent Capital.

Key Points:

  • A $20 billion US-Argentina currency swap has been finalized.
  • The agreement was confirmed by Michael Bessent of Bessent Capital.
