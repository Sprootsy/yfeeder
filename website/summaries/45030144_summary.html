Why do people keep writing about the imaginary compound Cr2Gr2Te6?

Summary

The article discusses the correction of a mistake in a previous article regarding the chemical formula of a certain material. Initially, the author incorrectly stated the formula as Cr2Gr2Te6. However, after receiving clarification and reviewing the experimental data, the author realized the correct formula is Cr2Ge2Te6. The author emphasizes the importance of accuracy in scientific writing and the need to correct errors promptly. The blog post serves as an erratum, rectifying the incorrect formula in the original article.

Key Points

  • The correct chemical formula is Cr2Ge2Te6, not Cr2Gr2Te6.
