Shutting Down Clear Linux OS

The Clear Linux* OS project is being discontinued. Intel, the driving force behind the project, has decided to end its investment in Clear Linux* OS. While the specific reasons for this decision are not explicitly stated, the announcement mentions a shift in Intel's strategy. The project will be shut down, and no further development or updates will be provided after a final release in December 2024. The community is invited to fork the project if they wish to continue its development independently.

Key Points:

  • Clear Linux* OS is being discontinued by Intel.
  • The project will be shut down after a final release in December 2024.
  • No further updates or development will occur after the final release.
  • The community is welcome to fork the project.
  • The decision is part of a change in Intel's strategy.
