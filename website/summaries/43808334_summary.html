Amazon Just Happens to Hold Book Sale During Independent Bookstore Day

According to the Gizmodo article, Amazon coincidentally held a major book sale that coincided with Independent Bookstore Day. Independent Bookstore Day is an annual event that celebrates independent bookstores and encourages people to shop at them. The timing of Amazon's sale has raised eyebrows, with many suspecting that it was intentionally scheduled to undermine the efforts of independent bookstores. The article highlights the ongoing competition between Amazon and independent bookstores, where Amazon's scale and pricing strategies often put independent bookstores at a disadvantage. The author suggests that supporting independent bookstores is important for maintaining a diverse and vibrant literary ecosystem.
