A small web July

This article discusses the author's reflections on participating in "A Small Web July," an initiative promoting personal websites and independent online spaces. The author shares their experiences and insights on creating and maintaining their own website, highlighting the benefits of having a digital space free from algorithms and corporate control. The author reflects on the joy of crafting a personalized online presence, connecting with others in a more authentic way, and having full ownership over their content. The article emphasizes the importance of fostering a diverse and decentralized web, encouraging readers to explore and contribute to the small web movement.
