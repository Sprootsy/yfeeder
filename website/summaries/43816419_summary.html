Show HN: I made a web-based, free alternative to Screen Studio

This article promotes Screen Recorder, a free online tool for recording computer screens and webcams. It emphasizes the software's ease of use, requiring no downloads or installations, and its accessibility across various devices, including Windows, Mac, Chromebook, and Linux systems. The recorder allows users to capture their screen, webcam, or both simultaneously, and it supports recording with or without audio. It provides options for selecting the recording area (full screen, application window, or Chrome tab) and offers features like microphone input and system sound recording. Once recording is complete, users can preview the video and download it in WebM format. The article highlights the tool's various applications, such as creating tutorials, recording gameplay, and capturing online meetings. It assures users of privacy, stating that recordings are processed locally within the browser and are not uploaded to any servers.

Key Points:

  • Screen Recorder is a free, online screen and webcam recording tool.
  • No download or installation is required.
  • It works on Windows, Mac, Chromebook, and Linux.
  • Users can record screen, webcam, or both.
  • Audio recording is supported (microphone and system sound).
  • Users can select the recording area (full screen, application window, or Chrome tab).
  • Recordings can be previewed and downloaded in WebM format.
  • Use cases include creating tutorials, recording gameplay, and capturing meetings.
  • Recordings are processed locally and are private.
