Guideline has been acquired by Gusto

Summary:

Guideline, a 401(k) provider, has been acquired by Gusto, a payroll and HR technology company. This acquisition aims to provide small businesses with a more integrated and comprehensive platform for managing payroll, benefits, and HR. The FAQ addresses common questions regarding the acquisition, emphasizing that Guideline will continue to operate as a separate entity and brand under the Gusto umbrella. There are no immediate changes to Guideline's pricing, services, or support. Existing Guideline customers will continue to be supported by the same Guideline team. The acquisition is intended to bring long-term benefits through product integration and enhanced services, but these developments will be rolled out gradually. Customers are assured that their 401(k) plans and data remain secure. The FAQ also covers potential future integrations between Guideline and Gusto, highlighting the intention to streamline administrative tasks for small businesses. The overall message is one of continuity and stability, with a focus on future improvements and a commitment to serving small business clients.

Key Points:

  • Guideline has been acquired by Gusto.
  • Guideline will continue to operate as a separate brand.
  • No immediate changes to pricing, services, or support.
  • Existing customers will continue to be supported by the same Guideline team.
  • The acquisition aims to create a more integrated platform for payroll, benefits, and HR.
  • Future product integrations are planned.
  • Customer 401(k) plans and data remain secure.
  • The acquisition intends to streamline administrative tasks for small businesses.
