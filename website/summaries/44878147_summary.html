Claude Sonnet 4 now supports 1M tokens of context

Summary:

The article announces that Anthropic is offering a 1 million context window for Claude, their AI assistant. This significantly expands the amount of information Claude can process and remember, moving from 9,000 tokens just a year ago, to 200,000 tokens and now 1 million tokens. Anthropic believes this advancement unlocks new possibilities for businesses and developers.

The extended context window enables Claude to work with vast amounts of information, essentially allowing it to analyze and understand entire codebases, lengthy financial reports, or extended literary works. This increased capacity allows for more in-depth analysis, more nuanced understanding, and the ability to recall information from earlier in the interaction, even when processing a massive document.

The article highlights several potential use cases. It can enable financial institutions to analyze massive datasets, legal teams to manage extensive discovery documents, and researchers to explore large volumes of research papers. Specifically, they mention that a user was able to upload the entire text of "The Great Gatsby" and ask Claude to identify character conflicts, which it did in 22 seconds. It also allows for use cases such as analyzing an entire company's code base, which was demonstrated with a codebase of ~173,000 lines of code.

Anthropic claims that they have achieved improvements in recall accuracy within this large context window. They also acknowledge the potential for models to lose information in very long contexts and claim that they have made progress in mitigating this issue with Claude.

Anthropic is offering the 1 million context window to a limited set of customers, and it is available in Claude's API. They are also making it available to Claude Pro users. The piece concludes by outlining Anthropic's vision for the future of AI, emphasizing the importance of large context windows for enabling more sophisticated and useful AI applications, and expressing excitement about the possibilities this technology unlocks.

Key Points:

  • Anthropic is offering a 1 million token context window for Claude.
  • This allows Claude to process and remember significantly larger amounts of information.
  • It enables the analysis of entire codebases, lengthy financial reports, and extensive literary works.
  • Use cases include financial analysis, legal discovery, research exploration, and codebase understanding.
  • Anthropic claims improvements in recall accuracy within the extended context window.
  • The 1 million context window is available to select customers via API and Claude Pro users.
  • Anthropic views large context windows as crucial for the future of advanced AI applications.
