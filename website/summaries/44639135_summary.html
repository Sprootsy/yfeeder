12ft.io Taken Down

Summary

The News Media Alliance (NMA) is strongly criticizing 12ft.io, a service that bypasses paywalls on news websites, calling it blatant copyright infringement and a threat to the sustainability of online journalism. The NMA argues that 12ft.io undermines the investment news publishers make in creating original content by allowing users to access articles without subscribing. The service essentially steals content and deprives publishers of revenue needed to support their operations.

The NMA highlights the importance of subscriptions and advertising revenue for news organizations to continue providing quality journalism. By circumventing paywalls, 12ft.io disrupts the business model that supports news production. The organization emphasizes that news is not free to produce and that publishers rely on various revenue streams to cover the costs of reporting, editing, and distribution.

The NMA is advocating for the protection of intellectual property rights and urging users to support news organizations by subscribing to their services. They are exploring all available options to combat 12ft.io and similar services that infringe on copyright and threaten the future of journalism.

Key Points

  • 12ft.io is accused of copyright infringement: The service bypasses paywalls, allowing users to access copyrighted news content without paying.
  • Threat to online journalism: The NMA argues that 12ft.io undermines the financial viability of news organizations.
  • Revenue streams are crucial: Subscriptions and advertising are essential for funding news production.
  • News is not free: The NMA emphasizes the costs associated with creating and distributing news content.
  • Protection of intellectual property: The NMA is advocating for the protection of publishers' rights.
  • Call to support news organizations: Users are encouraged to subscribe to news services instead of using paywall circumvention tools.
  • Exploring options to combat infringement: The NMA is considering various actions against 12ft.io and similar services.
