OPA maintainers and Styra employees hired by Apple

The article is a note from Teemu, Tim, and Torin to the Open Policy Agent (OPA) community. It announces their departure from Styra and their transition away from their roles as maintainers of the OPA project. They reflect on their journey with OPA, highlighting its growth from a small project to a graduated CNCF project widely adopted across various organizations. They express gratitude to the community, including users, contributors, maintainers, and sponsoring organizations, for their support and contributions to OPA's success. They acknowledge the challenges of balancing different priorities within the community and express confidence in the project's future under the continued stewardship of the maintainers and the community. They thank Styra for its support of OPA and express excitement about pursuing new endeavors, while remaining connected to the OPA community. The note emphasizes the importance of community involvement in shaping OPA's direction.

Key points:

  • Teemu, Tim, and Torin are stepping down from their roles at Styra and as maintainers of the OPA project.
  • They reflect on OPA's growth and success as a graduated CNCF project.
  • They express gratitude to the OPA community for their contributions and support.
  • They acknowledge the challenges of balancing community priorities.
  • They express confidence in OPA's future and the ongoing stewardship of current maintainers.
  • They thank Styra for its support of OPA.
  • They are excited about pursuing new opportunities.
  • They will remain connected to the OPA community.
