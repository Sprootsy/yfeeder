Genergo: Propellantless space-propulsion system

Genergo, an Italian company, has reportedly built and flight-tested the world's first known propellantless space propulsion system. This system has been validated in orbit. The technology, developed over 15 years, allows spacecraft to maneuver without using traditional propellant, offering potentially unlimited operational life and reduced costs. The company claims that the system is scalable, applicable to various spacecraft sizes, and compatible with current space infrastructure. According to Genergo, the technology has undergone rigorous testing and validation, demonstrating its functionality and reliability in the harsh environment of space. The company envisions this technology as a game-changer for the space industry, enabling new missions and reducing the environmental impact of space activities.
