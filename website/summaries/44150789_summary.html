Ukraine destroys more than 40 military aircraft in drone attack deep in Russia

According to Ukrainian intelligence sources, Ukraine conducted an attack inside Russia, specifically targeting and destroying Russian military aircraft. The operation reportedly took place at an airfield, resulting in damage or destruction to several planes. While details remain limited and are still emerging, the attack signifies a notable escalation in Ukraine's ability to strike targets within Russian territory. This development has potential implications for the ongoing conflict, raising questions about the range and precision of Ukraine's weaponry, as well as Russia's air defense capabilities.

Key points:

  • Ukraine conducted an attack inside Russia.
  • The attack targeted Russian military aircraft at an airfield.
  • Several planes were reportedly damaged or destroyed.
  • The attack represents an escalation in Ukraine's ability to strike targets within Russia.
