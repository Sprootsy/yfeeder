Microsoft only lets you opt out of AI photo scanning 3x a year

Microsoft has begun testing face-recognizing AI for photos in OneDrive for some preview users. This feature will automatically group photos by the faces of the people in them, making it easier to find and organize photos of specific individuals.

Key Points

  • Microsoft is testing face-recognizing AI in OneDrive.
  • The feature is currently available to some preview users.
  • The AI will group photos by the faces of people in them.
  • This feature aims to improve photo organization and search.
