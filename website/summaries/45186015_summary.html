iPhone Air

Apple has announced the iPhone Air, a new iPhone model featuring a thin and light design. The iPhone Air boasts an edge-to-edge display with the thinnest borders ever on an iPhone, achieved through a new structural design that integrates components. It comes in five finishes and utilizes 100 percent recycled aluminum.

The device introduces the A18 Bionic chip, delivering faster performance and improved power efficiency. The advanced chip enhances camera capabilities, gaming experiences, and AI tasks. The iPhone Air also features an upgraded camera system with a 48MP Main camera and advanced image processing for improved photo and video quality in various lighting conditions.

The iPhone Air ships with iOS 19, which includes a redesigned interface, enhanced privacy features, and new customization options. The operating system also integrates advanced AI capabilities for intelligent suggestions and automation.

The device includes a next-generation battery technology that provides all-day battery life despite the phone's thin profile. It also has faster wireless connectivity, including 6G cellular and Wi-Fi 7 support.

The iPhone Air is available for pre-order starting Friday, September 12, and will be available in stores starting Friday, September 19.
