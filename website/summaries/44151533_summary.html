Ukraine destroys more than 40 military aircraft in drone attack deep in Russia

According to NPR, Ukraine has claimed responsibility for destroying Russian military aircraft in an attack inside Russia. The attack reportedly took place at an airfield, resulting in damage to or destruction of several planes. While details are still emerging, this marks a significant escalation in the conflict, demonstrating Ukraine's capability to strike targets within Russian territory.

Key points:

  • Ukraine claims responsibility for an attack inside Russia.
  • The attack targeted a Russian airfield.
  • Russian military aircraft were reportedly destroyed or damaged.
