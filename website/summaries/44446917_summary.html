Couchers is officially out of beta

Summary:

The article announces the release of Couchers.org v1, marking a significant milestone for the platform after years of development by volunteers. It emphasizes that Couchers.org is a non-profit, volunteer-driven hospitality exchange platform intended as a community-owned alternative to for-profit platforms. The post details the core features available in v1, which include a functioning hospitality exchange with profiles, search, request sending, and messaging. It also highlights the community features, such as city and country discussion forums, groups, events, and the ability to create and manage these community spaces. Key aspects like safety features, including a vouching system, safety references, and reporting mechanisms, are described. The announcement expresses gratitude to the many contributors who made the launch possible and outlines the future vision for Couchers.org, inviting users to join, contribute, and help shape the platform's development and direction.

Key Points:

  • Release of Couchers.org v1: The first official version of the Couchers.org platform has been released.
  • Non-profit and Volunteer-Driven: Couchers.org is presented as a non-profit, volunteer-run alternative to commercial hospitality exchange platforms.
  • Hospitality Exchange Features: v1 includes core features for hospitality exchange, such as user profiles, search functionality, request sending, and messaging.
  • Community Features: The platform provides community features like city and country forums, groups, and events. Users can create and manage these community spaces.
  • Safety Measures: Safety is prioritized through features like a vouching system, safety references, and reporting mechanisms.
  • Gratitude to Contributors: The announcement acknowledges and thanks the numerous volunteers who contributed to the platform's development.
  • Future Vision: The post outlines the future vision for Couchers.org, encouraging users to participate in the platform's growth and development.
