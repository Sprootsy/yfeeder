Tesla's German car sales more than halve in October as wider EV sales jump

Tesla's car sales in Germany experienced a significant drop in October, falling by more than half compared to the same period last year. Registration data revealed a sharp decrease, contrasting with the overall growth in electric vehicle sales in the country. While the German car market saw an increase in EV registrations, Tesla's performance stood out as an anomaly. This decline follows Tesla's recent price cuts and various demand-stimulating measures. Specific figures detail the extent of the sales downturn, emphasizing the challenges Tesla faces in maintaining its market share in a key European market.

Key Points:

  • Tesla's German car sales more than halved in October compared to the previous year.
  • The decline in Tesla's sales contrasts with the overall growth in electric vehicle registrations in Germany.
  • The sales drop occurred despite Tesla's recent price cuts.
