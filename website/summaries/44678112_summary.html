Alto turns Apple Notes into a website

The provided URL leads to the website for Alto, a company that offers a self-directed IRA platform designed to allow individuals to invest in alternative assets such as startups, real estate, venture capital, and private equity, using their retirement funds. Alto aims to simplify and streamline the process of investing in these alternative assets, which are typically more complex and less accessible than traditional investments like stocks and bonds.

Alto's platform integrates with various alternative investment platforms and custodians, providing users with a centralized dashboard to manage their self-directed IRA and investments. They offer different account types, including Alto IRA and Alto Crypto IRA, catering to various investment preferences. The platform emphasizes tax-advantaged investing, as gains within an IRA can grow tax-deferred or tax-free, depending on the type of IRA (Traditional or Roth).

One of the key features of Alto is its ease of use. The platform aims to reduce the paperwork and complexities associated with self-directed IRAs, making it more accessible to a wider range of investors. By automating much of the administrative work involved, Alto simplifies the process of funding investments, managing paperwork, and tracking performance. Alto also offers educational resources to help investors understand the benefits and risks of alternative investments, as well as the rules and regulations governing self-directed IRAs.

In summary, Alto provides a platform that enables individuals to invest in alternative assets through their retirement accounts, simplifying the process, and aiming to make alternative investments more accessible. It offers tax advantages, streamlined administration, and educational resources to support investors.
