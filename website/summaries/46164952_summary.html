WikiFlix: Full Movies Hosted on Wikimedia Commons

The article discusses WikiFlix, a project aimed at creating a free and open-source platform for streaming and archiving films. The project intends to leverage the Wikimedia infrastructure and collaborative editing model to build a comprehensive and accessible film resource.

Key Points:

  • Project Goal: WikiFlix aims to create a free and open-source platform for streaming and archiving films, similar to Wikipedia but for movies.
  • Wikimedia Infrastructure: The project proposes utilizing the existing Wikimedia infrastructure, including servers, software, and community expertise.
  • Collaborative Editing: WikiFlix envisions a community-driven platform where users can contribute to and edit film information, subtitles, and potentially even upload films under appropriate licenses.
  • Accessibility: A core principle of WikiFlix is to provide free and open access to films for everyone, regardless of their location or economic status.
  • Licensing: The project emphasizes the importance of using open and permissive licenses for films and related content to ensure their free distribution and reuse.
  • Challenges: The article acknowledges the challenges associated with creating such a platform, including copyright issues, technical infrastructure, and community building.
  • Potential Benefits: WikiFlix has the potential to become a valuable resource for film enthusiasts, educators, and researchers, providing a free and open alternative to commercial streaming services.
