AI will shrink Amazon's workforce in the coming years, CEO Jassy says

Summary:

According to a CNBC article published hypothetically on June 17, 2025, Amazon CEO Andy Jassy addressed the company's evolving relationship with artificial intelligence (AI) and its impact on the workforce. The article describes how Amazon has significantly integrated AI across its operations, from warehouse automation and delivery logistics to customer service and cloud computing services (AWS). Jassy acknowledged that AI has led to some job displacement but emphasized Amazon's commitment to retraining and upskilling its employees to adapt to new roles created by AI. He highlighted the creation of new positions in AI development, data science, and AI-related support services.

The article further details Amazon's investment in AI-focused education programs for its employees, including partnerships with universities and online learning platforms. These programs aim to equip workers with the skills needed to manage, maintain, and improve AI systems. Jassy also discussed the ethical considerations surrounding AI, particularly concerning bias and fairness. He outlined Amazon's efforts to develop AI models that are transparent, accountable, and free from discriminatory practices. The company has established an AI ethics board to oversee the development and deployment of AI technologies.

Key Points:

  • Amazon has extensively integrated AI into its operations, impacting various sectors like warehousing, logistics, customer service, and AWS.
  • AI adoption has resulted in some job displacement within Amazon.
  • Amazon is committed to retraining and upskilling its workforce to transition employees into new roles related to AI.
  • The company is investing in AI-focused education programs and partnerships with universities to provide employees with the necessary skills.
  • Amazon is addressing the ethical implications of AI, focusing on transparency, accountability, and fairness in AI model development.
  • An AI ethics board has been established to oversee the responsible deployment of AI technologies within the company.
