Mullvad: Shutting down our search proxy Leta

Summary:

Mullvad VPN is shutting down its search proxy, Leta, on October 18, 2024. Leta was launched in February 2023 as a privacy-focused search tool that proxied requests to various search engines (Brave Search, DuckDuckGo, and Mojeek) to protect users' IP addresses. Mullvad's rationale for discontinuing the service is that it is no longer aligned with their core mission. They believe that focusing on their primary VPN service and related tools will provide more value to their users in the long run. The blog post expresses that while Leta was a useful tool, maintaining it diverts resources from their main objectives.

Key Points:

  • Mullvad VPN is discontinuing its search proxy, Leta.
  • Leta will be shut down on October 18, 2024.
  • Leta was a privacy-focused tool that proxied search requests to protect users' IP addresses.
  • The decision to shut down Leta is based on a realignment of resources towards Mullvad's core VPN service.
  • Mullvad believes focusing on their primary services will provide more value to users.
