Farewell friends

Summary:

The article is a farewell message to the readers of Humbledollar.com, announcing the website's closure on May 31, 2024. The author reflects on the journey of creating and running the site for over six years, highlighting its mission to offer practical advice and insights on personal finance topics like saving, investing, and retirement. The author expresses gratitude to the readers for their support, contributions, and engagement, which he considered the most rewarding aspect of the project. He acknowledges the changing landscape of online content and the need to move on to new ventures. The author reflects on the community that formed around the site, emphasizing the value of shared learning and support.
