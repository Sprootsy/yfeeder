Trying to send a sticker in Steam Chat burned through a month of mobile data

The Reddit post on r/Steam discusses an issue where a user accidentally spent $1000 while trying to send a sticker in the Steam chat. The user intended to send a sticker that cost around $0.45 but inadvertently purchased the same sticker multiple times due to a presumed glitch or misunderstanding of the Steam interface, resulting in the exorbitant charge. Other users in the comments shared similar experiences and offered advice, ranging from contacting Steam Support for a refund to warning about potential accidental purchases with Steam's interface.

  • The user intended to purchase a sticker for around $0.45.
