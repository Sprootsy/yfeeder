The un-celebrity president: Jimmy Carter shuns riches, lives modestly (2018)

The article discusses Jimmy Carter's post-presidency life, focusing on his commitment to a simple, modest lifestyle in his hometown of Plains, Georgia. Despite having opportunities to amass wealth through book deals and speaking engagements like other former presidents, Carter has intentionally chosen to live without luxury. He continues to reside in the modest home he built before his presidency, drives his own car, and frequents local establishments like the Dairy Queen. Carter dedicates much of his time to the Carter Center, where he works on global health initiatives, election monitoring, and conflict resolution. He also teaches Sunday school and remains deeply involved in his local community. The article emphasizes Carter's dedication to service and his conscious decision to avoid the trappings of wealth and celebrity that often come with being a former president.
