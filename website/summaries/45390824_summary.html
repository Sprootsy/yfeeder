Elon Musk and Prince Andrew named in latest Epstein files release

The article reports on the release of new documents related to the Jeffrey Epstein case. These documents name Elon Musk and Prince Andrew. The released documents are part of a court filing from a 2015 lawsuit brought by Virginia Giuffre against Ghislaine Maxwell.

The documents contain deposition testimony, emails, and other records. Mentions of Elon Musk appear in the context of a statement made by Johanna Sjoberg, one of Epstein's accusers, who claimed she was asked to give Musk a massage. Musk has denied this claim on X, formerly known as Twitter.

Prince Andrew's name also appears in the documents, adding to the scrutiny he has already faced regarding his association with Epstein. The details surrounding Prince Andrew's mention are not elaborated upon in great detail in this article.

The release of these documents is part of an ongoing legal process, with more names expected to be revealed as the case progresses.
