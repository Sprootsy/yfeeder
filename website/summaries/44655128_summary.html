Earthquake Causes 2.5-Meter Ground Slip in First-Ever Footage

A recent earthquake in Turkey and Syria caused a significant ground slip, estimated at 2.5 meters, which has been captured on video for the first time. The footage reveals the sheer power of the seismic event and the devastating impact it had on the Earth's surface. The earthquake, which has resulted in widespread destruction and loss of life, also triggered observable geological changes. This ground slip is a testament to the earthquake's magnitude and provides valuable data for scientists studying seismic activity.

Key Points:

  • Earthquake in Turkey and Syria caused a 2.5-meter ground slip.
  • The ground slip was captured on video.
