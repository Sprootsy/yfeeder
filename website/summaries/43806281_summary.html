BART's Anime Mascots

Summary:

BART (Bay Area Rapid Transit) is celebrating anime with a special promotion, aiming to enhance the commuting experience for riders and engage with fans of Japanese animation. This initiative involves wrapping one of BART's train cars with anime-themed artwork, creating a visually appealing and unique mobile exhibit. This "Art on Wheels" project intends to bring joy and surprise to regular BART passengers. By showcasing anime art on a train car, BART hopes to create a memorable and enjoyable ride for its passengers, turning a mundane commute into an exciting experience, and also draw attention to the transit system.

Key Points:

  • BART is launching an anime-themed promotion.
  • One BART train car will be wrapped in anime-themed artwork.
  • The initiative aims to improve the commuting experience for riders.
  • The promotion targets anime fans.
  • The wrapped train car is part of an "Art on Wheels" project.
  • The goal is to create a memorable and enjoyable ride.
