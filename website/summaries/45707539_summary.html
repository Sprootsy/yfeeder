D2: Diagram Scripting Language

The D2 language is a modern diagramming language that prioritizes clarity, efficiency, and a structured approach to diagram creation. It distinguishes itself from traditional tools by employing a declarative syntax, where users define the desired structure and connections of a diagram rather than manually arranging elements. This approach simplifies the process and enables automatic layout and styling, reducing the time and effort required to create visually appealing diagrams. D2 supports various diagram types, including flowcharts, sequence diagrams, and entity-relationship diagrams.

One of D2's core design principles is its emphasis on readability and maintainability. The language is designed to be easy to learn and use, even for individuals without extensive programming experience. Its concise syntax and intuitive keywords make it straightforward to express complex diagram structures. Moreover, D2 promotes modularity through features like subdiagrams and imports, allowing users to break down large diagrams into smaller, more manageable components. This modular approach enhances code reusability and simplifies collaboration among multiple contributors.

D2 also offers a high degree of customization. Users can control the appearance of diagrams through style sheets and themes, ensuring consistency across multiple diagrams. D2 supports customizable layouts and styling, allowing users to tailor diagrams to meet specific needs.

Furthermore, D2 is designed to integrate seamlessly with existing development workflows. It offers command-line interface (CLI) tools for generating diagrams from D2 source code, and it supports various output formats, including SVG, PNG, and PDF. This flexibility allows users to incorporate diagrams into documentation, presentations, and other deliverables with ease.

Key points:

  • D2 is a declarative diagramming language focused on clarity and efficiency.
  • It uses a declarative syntax, allowing users to define the desired structure of a diagram.
  • D2 promotes readability and maintainability through its simple syntax and modular design.
  • It supports customization through style sheets and themes.
  • D2 integrates with existing workflows via CLI tools and multiple output formats.
