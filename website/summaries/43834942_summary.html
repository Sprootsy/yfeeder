Indian court orders blocking of Proton Mail

Summary:

According to the TechCrunch article dated April 29, 2025, an Indian court has ordered the blocking of ProtonMail within the country. The decision stems from a case involving concerns about illegal activities and privacy issues. While the specific details of the case aren't thoroughly elaborated in the summary provided, the court order mandates that Indian internet service providers (ISPs) block access to ProtonMail's services, impacting Indian users' ability to utilize the platform's encrypted email capabilities.

Key Points:

  • An Indian court has ordered the blocking of ProtonMail.
  • The blocking is due to concerns about illegal activities and privacy issues.
  • Indian ISPs are required to block access to ProtonMail.
