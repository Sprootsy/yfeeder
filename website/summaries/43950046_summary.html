Fandom Sells Giant Bomb to Independent Creators

Summary:

Fandom has sold Giant Bomb, a video game website and community, back to its co-founders Jeff Gerstmann and Vinny Caravella. The acquisition will transition ownership from Fandom to the original creators, effectively making Giant Bomb an independently operated entity again.

Key Points:

  • Giant Bomb has been sold by Fandom to Jeff Gerstmann and Vinny Caravella.
  • The sale returns Giant Bomb to independent ownership under its original co-founders.
