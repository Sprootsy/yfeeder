Waymo and Toyota outline partnership to advance autonomous driving deployment

Summary

Waymo and Toyota have announced a strategic partnership to explore the integration of Waymo Driver technology into Toyota vehicles. This collaboration aims to leverage Waymo's autonomous driving system with Toyota's manufacturing expertise and vehicle platforms to create autonomous vehicles at scale. The partnership focuses on understanding how Waymo Driver can enhance various transportation solutions, with potential applications across different vehicle types and business models. Both companies emphasize a commitment to safety and efficiency in advancing autonomous mobility.

Key Points

  • Waymo and Toyota are partnering to integrate Waymo Driver technology into Toyota vehicles.
  • The collaboration aims to create autonomous vehicles at scale by combining Waymo's technology with Toyota's manufacturing capabilities.
  • The partnership will explore various applications of Waymo Driver across different vehicle types and business models.
  • Both companies emphasize a commitment to safety and efficiency in advancing autonomous mobility solutions.
