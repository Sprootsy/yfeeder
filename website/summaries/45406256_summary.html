Denmark bans civil drones after more sightings

Summary:

Denmark has temporarily banned civilian drones following an increase in drone activity near critical infrastructure. The ban, effective immediately, aims to allow authorities to assess the threat and implement countermeasures. The Danish Transport Authority announced the measure, citing a heightened security situation and the need to protect vital sites. This decision follows similar concerns raised in Norway regarding drone sightings near oil and gas installations after explosions on the Nord Stream pipelines in the Baltic Sea in 2022.

Key Points:

  • Denmark has banned civilian drones.
  • The ban is temporary and effective immediately.
  • The reason is increased drone activity near critical infrastructure.
  • The purpose is to assess the threat and implement countermeasures.
