When will M&S take online orders again?

Summary:

The article discusses the ongoing issues customers are experiencing with online orders from Marks & Spencer (M&S). It highlights that numerous customers have taken to social media to complain about missing items, delayed deliveries, and a general lack of communication from M&S regarding their orders. The problems appear to be widespread, affecting a significant number of online shoppers. The issues range from individual items missing from orders to entire orders not being delivered at all. Customers are expressing frustration over the difficulty in contacting M&S customer service to resolve these problems. The article suggests that the issues may stem from problems within M&S's logistics and supply chain, potentially exacerbated by increased online shopping volumes. It is emphasized that while occasional issues are expected, the scale and persistence of these problems are damaging M&S's reputation for quality and customer service. The article implies that M&S needs to address these operational problems swiftly to retain customer trust and avoid long-term damage to its brand image. Furthermore, there is a discussion on possible compensation or resolution strategies for affected customers. The main sentiment conveyed is one of customer frustration and disappointment with M&S's recent online order fulfillment performance.
