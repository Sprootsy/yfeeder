Project Amplify: Powered footwear for running and walking

Nike's Project Amplify is a program designed to support and elevate Black designers and creators. The initiative provides resources, mentorship, and opportunities to help these individuals bring their unique visions to life within the footwear and apparel industry. The project aims to address historical disparities and create a more inclusive and equitable landscape within Nike and the broader design world.

The latest installment of Project Amplify showcases a new collection featuring designs from a cohort of emerging Black talent. The collection includes footwear and apparel that reflect the designers' personal stories, cultural influences, and innovative approaches to design. The products incorporate unique materials, patterns, and color palettes, demonstrating the designers' creativity and skill.

The designs are rooted in celebrating the Black experience, and challenging conventional norms. The project serves as a platform for these designers to share their perspectives and contribute to a more diverse and representative design ecosystem. The collaboration includes mentorship from established Nike designers and industry leaders, which provides invaluable guidance and support to the participants.

Nike is committed to fostering long-term relationships with the designers involved in Project Amplify, providing ongoing support and resources to help them advance their careers. The project represents a step towards creating a more equitable industry that values and celebrates diverse voices and perspectives. By investing in Black designers and creators, Nike aims to inspire future generations and drive positive change within the design community.
