France Endorses UN Open Source Principles

The article is a post on the Mastodon social network by the account codegouvfr, which is the account for the French government's open source program. The post announces the publication of a resource: a presentation providing a method for estimating the Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) of digital services. The presentation is available in French. The announcement encourages users to use and share the presentation, tagging @etalab in their posts. Etalab is a department within the French government responsible for open data and open government initiatives.

Key Points

  • Publication of a presentation detailing a method for estimating the TCO (Total Cost of Ownership) of digital services.
  • The resource is provided by the French government's open source program (codegouvfr).
  • The presentation is in French.
  • Users are encouraged to use and share the resource.
  • Users are asked to tag @etalab when sharing.
