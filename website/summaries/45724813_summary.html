Amazon targets as many as 30k corporate job cuts, sources say

According to sources, Amazon is aiming to cut as many as 30,000 corporate jobs across its various divisions, with the cuts potentially continuing into the first quarter of 2025. The job cuts are intended to reduce costs. The cuts are targeting various divisions within Amazon, including the devices and books units, advertising, and human resources.

Key points:

  • Amazon is planning to cut potentially 30,000 corporate jobs.
  • The cuts may continue into the first quarter of 2025.
  • The job cuts are intended to reduce costs.
  • The cuts will affect various divisions, including devices, books, advertising, and HR.
