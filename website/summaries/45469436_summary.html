Discord customer service data breach leaks user info and scanned photo IDs

Summary

Discord experienced a data breach involving a third-party customer support platform. The breach resulted in unauthorized access to the email addresses, usernames, and support tickets of some Discord users. Discord stated that payment information and passwords were not exposed, and the company has disabled the compromised customer support platform and is conducting an investigation.

Key Points

  • Discord experienced a data breach through a third-party customer support system.
  • Email addresses, usernames, and support tickets of some users were accessed.
  • Payment information and passwords were not exposed.
  • The compromised customer support platform has been disabled.
  • Discord is investigating the breach.
