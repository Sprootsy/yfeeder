OpenAI to buy AI startup from Jony Ive

Summary

According to a Bloomberg News report, OpenAI is poised to acquire Jony Ive's AI device startup in a deal valued at $6.5 billion. Jony Ive, a former Apple design chief, has been working on developing innovative AI devices. While specific details about the startup and the devices in development remain scarce, the acquisition signals OpenAI's ambition to expand beyond software and venture into the hardware space. This acquisition would bring Ive's design expertise to OpenAI, potentially revolutionizing how users interact with AI technology.

Key Points

  • OpenAI is reportedly acquiring Jony Ive's AI device startup.
  • The acquisition deal is valued at $6.5 billion.
  • Jony Ive is a former design chief at Apple.
  • The acquisition indicates OpenAI's move into hardware development.
