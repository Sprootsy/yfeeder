YASA beats own power density record pushing electric motor to 59kW/kg benchmark

Summary

YASA, a Mercedes-Benz company, has announced that it has broken its own unofficial world record for power density in electric motors, achieving a staggering 5.9 kW/kg. This new benchmark was achieved by pushing the state-of-the-art of electric motor technology. The company focuses on developing advanced electric drive solutions for automotive and other sectors.

Key Points

  • YASA has broken its own power density world record for electric motors.
  • The new record is 5.9 kW/kg.
  • YASA is a Mercedes-Benz company.
  • YASA develops advanced electric drive solutions.
