Corporation for Public Broadcasting Statement Regarding Executive Order

The Corporation for Public Broadcasting (CPB) has released a statement addressing the Executive Order regarding public media. The statement emphasizes CPB's commitment to its mission, which is to support public media's role in providing valuable educational and informational programming and services to the American public. CPB highlights its unique role as a steward of federal funding, ensuring that public media remains independent from political influence. The organization underscores its dedication to localism, providing support to local public media stations that understand and serve the unique needs of their communities. CPB also champions the importance of public media's accessibility to all Americans, regardless of their location or socioeconomic status, offering content on various platforms, including television, radio, and digital media. The statement reaffirms CPB's commitment to transparency and accountability in its operations and financial management.

Key Points:

  • CPB reaffirms its commitment to its mission of supporting public media.
  • CPB emphasizes its role in ensuring public media's independence from political influence.
  • CPB highlights its dedication to supporting local public media stations.
  • CPB champions the accessibility of public media for all Americans.
  • CPB reaffirms its commitment to transparency and accountability.
