Ask HN: What are you working on? (September 2025)

Summary

The article is about Apple's unveiling of its new AI strategy, dubbed "Apple Intelligence," at its Worldwide Developers Conference (WWDC). It details how Apple is integrating AI deeply into its operating systems (iOS 18, iPadOS 18, and macOS Sequoia) while emphasizing user privacy. Apple Intelligence aims to enhance user experience through features like improved Siri, writing tools, image generation, and more context-aware notifications. A core aspect of Apple's approach is on-device processing for many AI tasks, ensuring data stays on the user's device. For more complex requests, Apple introduces "Private Cloud Compute," which uses Apple silicon servers to process data in a privacy-preserving manner. Apple emphasizes that this system undergoes independent inspection to verify its security claims. The article further explores specific features powered by Apple Intelligence. Siri is receiving a significant upgrade, becoming more natural, contextually aware, and capable of handling complex tasks across apps. New writing tools will help users rewrite, proofread, and summarize text in various applications. Image generation capabilities are also being introduced, allowing users to create images from sketches or text prompts. Other features include smart replies in Mail, improved search functionality, and more relevant notifications. The article also mentions Apple's partnership with OpenAI to integrate ChatGPT into its ecosystem. Users will be able to access ChatGPT's capabilities through Siri, with Apple emphasizing privacy protections for this integration. Finally, the article touches on the system requirements for Apple Intelligence. It will be available on iPhone 15 Pro and later models, as well as iPads and Macs with M1 chips or later. The initial release will be in US English, with other languages to follow.

Key Points

  • Apple Intelligence: Apple's new AI strategy focused on integrating AI deeply into its operating systems (iOS 18, iPadOS 18, and macOS Sequoia).
  • Privacy Focus: Emphasizes on-device processing and "Private Cloud Compute" for data privacy.
  • Enhanced Siri: Significant Siri upgrade with improved natural language understanding and cross-app functionality.
  • Writing Tools: New tools for rewriting, proofreading, and summarizing text.
  • Image Generation: Ability to create images from sketches or text prompts.
  • ChatGPT Integration: Partnership with OpenAI to integrate ChatGPT into Siri, with privacy protections.
  • System Requirements: Available on iPhone 15 Pro and later, and iPads/Macs with M1 or later. Initial release in US English.
