This document is a listing of top-level domains (TLDs) as maintained by the Internet Assigned Numbers Authority (IANA). The purpose of this document is to serve as a definitive and up-to-date registry for internet addressing, ensuring that software and systems that manage domain names have a consistent and accurate view of the TLD landscape.
