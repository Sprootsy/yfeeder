Project Shadowglass

Summary

ShadowGlass is a cooperative tabletop miniatures game designed for 1-4 players. It blends elements of dungeon crawling, tower defense, and resource management within a dark fantasy setting. Players take on the roles of Shadowforged, beings imbued with the power of the fallen goddess Lunessa, and must defend the last shard of her essence, the Ebonheart, from the encroaching forces of the Rot. The game utilizes miniatures to represent the Shadowforged and the Rotspawn, with gameplay taking place on modular tiles that create a variety of environments.

Players choose from several unique Shadowforged characters, each possessing distinct abilities, strengths, and playstyles. They must strategically position themselves on the game board, utilizing their abilities and the environment to slow down and defeat the Rotspawn. A key aspect of ShadowGlass is the resource management system, where players collect Soul Shards dropped by defeated enemies. These shards are crucial for activating powerful character abilities and constructing defenses to bolster their position.

The game progresses through a series of encounters, each presenting different challenges and enemy compositions. As the Rotspawn advance, players must adapt their tactics and coordinate their actions to prevent the Ebonheart from being corrupted. Successfully completing encounters rewards players with experience, which can be used to upgrade their Shadowforged characters and unlock new abilities.

ShadowGlass emphasizes cooperative gameplay, requiring players to work together to overcome the odds. Effective communication, strategic planning, and resource allocation are essential for survival. The game also features a branching narrative, allowing players to make choices that influence the story and the challenges they face. The ultimate goal is to protect the Ebonheart and hold back the tide of the Rot, ensuring the hope of Lunessa's return remains alive.
