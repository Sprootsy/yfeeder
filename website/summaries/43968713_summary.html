FedRAMP 20x – One Month in and Moving Fast

Summary

The article discusses the progress of FedRAMP (Federal Risk and Authorization Management Program) in achieving its "20x in 2020" goal, which aimed to authorize 20 cloud service offerings (CSOs) by the end of 2020. While the specific date in the title refers to a date in April, the article highlights accomplishments made in the month following that date. The announcement states that the FedRAMP PMO (Program Management Office) continues to actively engage with cloud service providers (CSPs) and federal agencies to streamline the authorization process and foster a more efficient and secure cloud environment for the U.S. government. FedRAMP is seeing good progress towards its goal.

Key Points

  • Goal: FedRAMP's primary objective is to authorize 20 cloud service offerings (CSOs) under the “20x in 2020” initiative.
  • Progress: The PMO is actively working with CSPs and agencies.
