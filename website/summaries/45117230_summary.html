Nuclear: Desktop music player focused on streaming from free sources

Nuclear is a free and open-source music streaming program that pulls content from multiple sources, including YouTube, SoundCloud, Bandcamp, and local files. It is available for Windows, macOS, and Linux.

Key points:

  • Nuclear is a music streaming program.
  • It is free and open-source.

  • It is available for Windows, macOS, and Linux.
