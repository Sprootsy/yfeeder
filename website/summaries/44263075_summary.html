US Senator Alex Padilla forcibly removed from Kristi Noem news conference

According to an NBC Los Angeles article, California U.S. Senator Alex Padilla has criticized South Dakota Governor Kristi Noem for her comments regarding potential protests in Los Angeles should Donald Trump be indicted. Noem stated on social media that she was sending the South Dakota National Guard to Los Angeles, prompting swift backlash. Padilla described Noem's statement as dangerous and irresponsible, accusing her of trying to incite violence and fear. He emphasized that decisions about law enforcement and public safety in California are the responsibility of California authorities. He also pointed out the lack of evidence suggesting an imminent threat that would warrant such an intervention from another state's National Guard. The article does not indicate any formal request or coordination with California officials regarding Noem's offer.
