Dog Walk: Blender Studio's official game project

DogWalk is a short, experimental 3D animated film created by Blender Studio. It explores themes of grief, letting go, and moving on. The film is about a woman named Maya who confronts the loss of her beloved dog, Blue. Maya walks through familiar places with Blue's empty collar, revisiting memories and dealing with her emotions. The project served as a testbed for new features and workflows in Blender, focusing on character design, rigging, animation, and real-time rendering using Eevee. The short aims to be both technically innovative and emotionally resonant.

Key points:

  • Short Film: DogWalk is a short animated film by Blender Studio.
  • Themes: The film deals with grief, letting go, and moving on after the loss of a pet.
  • Character: The story centers around a woman named Maya who mourns the loss of her dog, Blue.
  • Plot: Maya walks with Blue's collar, revisiting places they shared and confronting her grief.
  • Technical Experiment: The project was used to test new features and workflows in Blender.
  • Focus Areas: The project emphasized character design, rigging, animation, and real-time rendering with Eevee.
