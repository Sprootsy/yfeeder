ROG Xbox Ally runs better on Linux than Windows it ships with

According to a recent test, the ROG Ally handheld gaming device performs better on Linux than on the Windows operating system it comes with. The tests revealed up to 32% higher FPS (frames per second) with more stable framerates and quicker sleep/resume times when running on Linux.

Key Points:

  • The ROG Ally shows performance improvements on Linux compared to Windows.
  • Linux exhibited up to 32% higher FPS in gaming benchmarks.
  • Framerates were more stable on Linux.
  • Sleep/resume times were faster on Linux.
