Caltrain official lived in secret apartment built illegally inside train station

Summary:

According to an article by The San Francisco Standard, a Caltrain employee has been living in a secret apartment hidden within the Burlingame station for an unknown period. The article includes photographs showing the interior of the apartment, which is reportedly located behind a locked door and is furnished with amenities such as a bed, refrigerator, and television. The discovery raises questions about the employee's potential misuse of public resources and possible violations of Caltrain's policies regarding employee housing and facility usage. Caltrain is reportedly investigating the situation to determine the full extent of the employee's actions and whether any misconduct occurred.
