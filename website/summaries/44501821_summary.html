GlobalFoundries to Acquire MIPS

Summary

According to a press release, GlobalFoundries (GF) and MIPS, a provider of RISC-V processor architectures and IP, are partnering to deliver MIPS RISC-V IP on GF's 22FDX platform. This collaboration aims to provide optimized solutions for a variety of applications, including automotive, industrial, and consumer IoT devices.

The partnership combines the power efficiency and feature-rich capabilities of GF's 22FDX platform with MIPS's high-performance RISC-V processor IP. The 22FDX platform is known for its low power consumption, making it suitable for battery-powered devices and energy-conscious applications. MIPS, on the other hand, brings its expertise in RISC-V architecture to the table, offering scalable and customizable processor solutions. By integrating MIPS RISC-V IP on GF's 22FDX platform, the collaboration seeks to provide customers with a competitive edge in terms of performance, power efficiency, and cost-effectiveness.

The press release suggests that the collaboration will enable the development of innovative solutions for a range of applications. In the automotive sector, the combined offering can power advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) and in-vehicle infotainment (IVI) systems. In the industrial sector, it can enable intelligent control systems and edge computing devices. For consumer IoT devices, the partnership aims to facilitate the development of smart home devices, wearables, and other connected devices.

The press release highlights the benefits of the partnership for customers. By leveraging GF's 22FDX platform and MIPS' RISC-V IP, customers can achieve faster time-to-market, reduced development costs, and improved product performance. The collaboration also provides access to a comprehensive ecosystem of tools, software, and support, further simplifying the development process.
