Open models by OpenAI

Summary:

The article discusses OpenAI's open-source models. It highlights several open-source models released by OpenAI, including Point-E, Whisper, and Triton. Point-E is a system for generating 3D models from text prompts. Whisper is an automatic speech recognition system. Triton is a programming language designed to write efficient GPU code. The article emphasizes OpenAI's commitment to releasing open-source technologies to benefit the broader AI community and advance research. These open-source releases enable researchers and developers to use, study, modify, and distribute these technologies, fostering innovation and collaboration in the field of artificial intelligence.
