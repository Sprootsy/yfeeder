19% of California houses are owned by investors

Summary

According to a report by the California Association of Realtors, 19% of California houses are owned by investors. This ownership rate is based on 2021 data, which shows a significant portion of the housing market being controlled by entities other than individual homeowners. Investor activity can impact the availability and affordability of homes for traditional buyers, potentially driving up prices and reducing the inventory of homes for sale.

Key Points

  • 19% of California houses are owned by investors.
  • The data is based on 2021 figures.
  • Investor activity can impact the availability and affordability of homes.
