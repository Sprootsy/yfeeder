BYD builds fastest car

Summary:

The article discusses BYD's unveiling of the Yangwang U9, an all-electric supercar, which the company claims is the world's fastest EV. The U9 boasts impressive specifications, including a quad-motor powertrain producing over 1,300 horsepower, enabling it to accelerate from 0 to 62 mph (0-100 km/h) in a claimed 2.36 seconds. It features BYD's Disus-X suspension system, offering advanced handling capabilities and the ability to drive on three wheels. The car's design incorporates aerodynamic elements and a luxurious interior. While pricing details for markets outside of China haven't been released, the U9 is currently priced at 1.68 million yuan (approximately £185,000) in China. The article highlights BYD's ambitions to compete in the high-performance electric vehicle market, showcasing the U9 as a symbol of the company's technological advancements and design prowess.
