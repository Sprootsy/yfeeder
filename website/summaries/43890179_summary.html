The Signal Clone the Trump Admin Uses Was Hacked

Summary:

An encrypted messaging app called "Signal Clone," used by officials in the Trump administration, was hacked. This version of Signal was not officially supported by Signal, and it was maintained by the US government. It's unclear exactly who was affected by the hack. The vulnerabilities were identified in the Android version of the app.

Key Points:

  • A Signal clone, used by the Trump administration, was hacked.
  • The app was not officially supported by Signal.
  • The hacked version of the app ran on Android.
