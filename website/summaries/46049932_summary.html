A new bridge links the math of infinity to computer science

The article discusses a newly discovered connection between the mathematical concept of infinity and theoretical computer science, specifically relating to the field of program verification. This hierarchy of infinities, characterized by cardinal numbers, has intrigued mathematicians for generations.

Program verification is the process of formally proving that a computer program will behave as intended under all possible circumstances, a notoriously difficult problem.
