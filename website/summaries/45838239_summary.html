OpenDesk is a global platform that connects customers with a network of independent furniture makers to produce and deliver locally-made, customizable furniture. They offer a range of designs, including desks, tables, chairs, and storage solutions, that are suitable for both home and office environments. The platform emphasizes sustainable and ethical production by leveraging local manufacturing capabilities and using responsibly sourced materials.

The OpenDesk model aims to disrupt the traditional furniture industry by offering an alternative to mass-produced, globally-shipped items. Customers can browse the OpenDesk catalog, select a design, customize it to their specific needs (in some cases), and then connect with a local maker who will produce the furniture. This process reduces transportation costs and environmental impact, supports local economies, and provides customers with high-quality, bespoke furniture.

The company emphasizes collaboration and open-source design. Many of their designs are created by independent designers and made available on the platform under open licenses. This allows for continuous improvement and adaptation of the designs based on user feedback. OpenDesk also works with businesses and organizations to create custom furniture solutions for their specific needs, further highlighting the platform's flexibility.

The website, available in German, provides information on the furniture designs, the making process, the materials used (often focusing on sustainable options like plywood), and the network of makers. It also includes case studies and testimonials from satisfied customers, demonstrating the value and benefits of using the OpenDesk platform. OpenDesk promotes a vision of distributed manufacturing and a more sustainable, localized furniture industry.
