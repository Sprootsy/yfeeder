Israel launches strikes against Iran, Defense Minister says

According to CNN, there were reports of explosions in Iran and Syria. Sources familiar with the matter told CNN that Israel carried out strikes inside Iran. Iranian state media reported explosions in Isfahan, attributing them to air defense systems intercepting drones. There were no reports of a missile strike. An Iranian official stated that there are no plans for immediate retaliation. Details regarding the targets are still emerging. ABC News reported that a US official confirmed Israeli missiles hit a site in Iran. There were also reports of strikes in southern Syria, according to the Syrian Observatory for Human Rights.
