I Found Malware in a BeamNG Mod

Summary:

The article is a cautionary tale about a specific instance of malware distributed through the BeamNG.drive modding community. It details how a user downloaded a mod for the game from an unofficial source and inadvertently infected their system with a password-stealing virus. The article walks through the user's experience, from initial suspicion due to unusual system behavior, to the eventual confirmation of the malware's presence via a comprehensive system scan. It emphasizes the dangers of downloading mods from untrusted sources, highlighting the potential for seemingly harmless game modifications to contain malicious code. The article serves as a warning to the BeamNG.drive community and gamers in general, urging them to exercise caution and prioritize security when downloading and installing mods.
