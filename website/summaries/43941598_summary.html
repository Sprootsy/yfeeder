Fleurs du Mal

The website Fleursdumal.org is an online archive dedicated to Charles Baudelaire's renowned poetry collection, *Les Fleurs du Mal* (The Flowers of Evil). The site provides access to the original French text of the poems. The website offers more than just the original French text, though. Users can also access multiple English translations of Baudelaire's work, allowing for a comparative study of the poem across languages and translator interpretations. The site aims to provide resources for students, scholars, and general readers interested in exploring Baudelaire's poetry in depth.

Key Points:

  • Fleursdumal.org is a website dedicated to Charles Baudelaire's *Les Fleurs du Mal*.
  • The site features the original French text of the poems.
  • Multiple English translations are available.
  • The website serves as a resource for studying Baudelaire's poetry.
