Show HN: NYC Subway Simulator and Route Designer

Summary

The website buildmytransit.nyc presents a vision for a transformed New York City transit system, focusing on expansion and modernization. It advocates for a comprehensive approach to improving public transportation to meet the needs of a growing and evolving city. The core idea is to build new subway lines, extend existing ones, and integrate these expansions with other modes of transportation, such as buses and ferries, to create a seamless and efficient network.

The site highlights several key proposed projects and expansions. One major focus is on expanding subway access to underserved areas, particularly in the outer boroughs, aiming to reduce commute times and improve access to jobs and opportunities for residents. This includes extending existing lines like the 2nd Avenue Subway further into Manhattan and the Bronx, as well as building entirely new lines to connect neighborhoods that currently lack adequate subway service.

Modernization is another crucial aspect of the vision. The website emphasizes the need to upgrade existing infrastructure, including replacing aging tracks and signals, improving station accessibility, and implementing modern technology to enhance safety and efficiency. This modernization is seen as essential for ensuring the long-term reliability and performance of the transit system.

The plan also addresses the importance of integrating different modes of transportation. The goal is to create a unified system where subways, buses, and ferries work together seamlessly. This includes improving bus routes and frequency, creating better connections between buses and subways, and expanding ferry service to provide additional transportation options, especially in waterfront communities.

Funding is, of course, a significant consideration, and the website touches on potential funding mechanisms for these ambitious projects. It suggests a combination of federal, state, and local funding, as well as innovative financing approaches, to make these transit improvements a reality.

Ultimately, buildmytransit.nyc envisions a future where New York City has a world-class transit system that supports economic growth, reduces congestion, and improves the quality of life for all residents. It calls for a bold and comprehensive approach to transit planning and investment to meet the challenges of a growing metropolis.

Key Points

  • Advocates for a comprehensive expansion and modernization of the NYC transit system.
  • Proposes building new subway lines and extending existing ones to underserved areas.
  • Emphasizes the importance of upgrading existing infrastructure and implementing modern technology.
  • Focuses on integrating subways, buses, and ferries to create a seamless transportation network.
  • Addresses the need for a combination of federal, state, and local funding for transit projects.
  • Aims to create a world-class transit system that supports economic growth and improves quality of life.
