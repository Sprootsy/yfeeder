Webflow's blog post announces that GSAP (GreenSock Animation Platform), a popular JavaScript animation library, is now free for commercial use. Previously, a paid license was required for commercial projects using certain GSAP plugins. This change removes that barrier, making the full suite of GSAP tools accessible to all developers, regardless of their project's nature or revenue. The announcement highlights the benefits of using GSAP, emphasizing its performance, flexibility, and cross-browser compatibility. The move to a completely free model aims to encourage wider adoption of GSAP within the web development community, allowing more creators to leverage its powerful animation capabilities without cost concerns. It also emphasizes the continued development and support for GSAP, ensuring its longevity and relevance in the evolving landscape of web animation.
