FSE meets the FBI

Summary: The article, published on freespeechextremist.com, details the author's experiences with and perspective on the FBI's interactions with him. The piece alleges that the FBI has been monitoring him and his activities for an extended period, citing various incidents of surveillance and contact, including agents visiting his residence and questioning his associates. He contends that his platform serves as an important outlet for diverse and often dissenting voices, and he accuses the FBI of overreach in their monitoring of his activities. The article also touches on themes of government surveillance, censorship, and the importance of protecting free speech, even when the views expressed are unpopular or controversial. He also suggests the FBI's focus on him is a misallocation of resources that could be better used to address genuine threats to national security.
