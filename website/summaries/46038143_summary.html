PS5 now costs less than 64GB of DDR5 memory. RAM jumps to $600 due to shortage

A 64GB kit of DDR5 memory now costs more than an entire PlayStation 5, even after a discount. The Trident Z5 Neo kit has jumped to USD 600 due to a DRAM shortage, and it is expected to get worse into 2026.

Key points:

  • The price of DDR5 memory is increasing significantly due to a DRAM shortage.
  • A 64GB DDR5 memory kit now costs more than a PlayStation 5.
  • The DRAM shortage is expected to worsen into 2026.
