Ground stop at JFK due to staffing

Summary

The ATCSCC ADVZY 013 advisory, concerning JFK/ZNY (John F. Kennedy International Airport/New York Air Route Traffic Control Center), details a CDM (Collaborative Decision Making) GROUND STOP implemented on October 31, 2025. The advisory was issued due to STAFFING/VOLUME issues. The advisory specifies a Ground Stop with an expected start time of 1500Z and an estimated end time of 1700Z.

Key Points

  • Advisory Number: ATCSCC ADVZY 013
  • Affected Area: JFK/ZNY
  • Date: October 31, 2025
  • Reason: STAFFING/VOLUME
  • Type of Action: CDM GROUND STOP
  • Expected Start Time: 1500Z
  • Estimated End Time: 1700Z
