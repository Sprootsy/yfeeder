Report: Tim Cook could step down as Apple CEO 'as soon as next year'

According to a recent report, Tim Cook might step down as Apple's CEO as soon as next year. While Apple has been preparing for Cook's eventual departure for some time, the exact timing has remained uncertain. The report suggests that Cook may be considering leaving the role after overseeing the launch of several key new products, including the Apple Car and the next generation of AR glasses. The article also mentions potential candidates to replace Cook, highlighting both internal executives and external figures with the skills and experience to lead Apple.

Key Points:

  • Tim Cook is potentially stepping down as Apple's CEO as early as next year.
  • Apple has been preparing for Cook's departure.
  • Cook may depart after launching the Apple Car and next-generation AR glasses.
  • The report suggests potential internal and external candidates to replace Cook.
