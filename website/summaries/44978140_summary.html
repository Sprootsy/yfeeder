Summary:

The splice-cad.com website presents Splice, a cloud-native Electronic Design Automation (EDA) platform. Splice is designed to accelerate PCB design and manufacturing through its collaborative environment. It aims to provide designers with tools to streamline and accelerate the PCB design process.

Splice CAD emphasizes its features such as multi-user collaboration, a vast component library, real-time supply chain data, and integration with manufacturing processes.

The platform focuses on reducing design errors, facilitating team collaboration, and shortening the time-to-market for electronic products. Its cloud-based nature allows for accessibility from any location and eliminates the need for complex software installations.
