Plain Vanilla Web

Summary:

The plainvanillaweb.com website is a personal site belonging to Karl Dubost. It serves as a digital garden where he cultivates notes, ideas, and resources related to various topics, primarily web development, open standards, accessibility, and sustainable web practices. The site's design philosophy emphasizes simplicity, accessibility, and sustainability, reflecting Dubost's commitment to a user-friendly and environmentally conscious web experience.

The content on the site is diverse, encompassing articles, tutorials, presentations, and links to external resources. Dubost shares his expertise and insights on topics such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, web accessibility (WCAG), responsive web design, and the importance of using open standards. He also delves into more philosophical aspects of web development, advocating for ethical and sustainable practices that minimize environmental impact and promote inclusivity.

A significant focus of the site is promoting web standards and best practices. Dubost actively encourages developers to adhere to W3C standards, ensuring interoperability and accessibility across different browsers and devices. He provides practical guidance and examples to help developers implement these standards effectively.

Accessibility is another core theme, with Dubost emphasizing the need to create websites that are usable by everyone, including people with disabilities. He provides resources and advice on how to implement WCAG guidelines, making websites more inclusive and accessible.

Furthermore, the site addresses the environmental impact of web development. Dubost advocates for sustainable web practices, such as optimizing images, reducing code bloat, and choosing energy-efficient hosting providers. He encourages developers to consider the environmental consequences of their work and to adopt strategies to minimize their carbon footprint.

The overall tone of the site is informative, educational, and opinionated. Dubost shares his personal experiences and perspectives, offering practical advice and thought-provoking insights. The site's design is deliberately simple and minimalist, reflecting its commitment to sustainability and user-friendliness. The website is powered by Frog CMS.

plainvanillaweb.com can be seen as a valuable resource for web developers, designers, and anyone interested in learning more about web standards, accessibility, and sustainable web practices. It provides a wealth of information, practical guidance, and thought-provoking perspectives on how to build a better web.
