ICANN fee price hike by 11% [pdf]

Summary

ICANN is planning a variable accreditation fee adjustment for registrars, set to take effect on January 1, 2025. This adjustment aims to ensure sufficient funding for ICANN's operations, security obligations, and to maintain a financially stable organization. The proposed fee adjustment is intended to address the projected financial needs outlined in ICANN's FY25 Operating Plan and Budget. The announcement emphasizes ICANN's commitment to transparency and invites registrars to review the details and supporting documentation to understand the rationale behind the proposed adjustment. Registrars are encouraged to assess the potential impact on their businesses. ICANN plans to hold informational sessions to discuss the fee adjustment and answer questions from registrars.
