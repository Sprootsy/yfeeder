Summary

Seattle Ultrasonics is a company specializing in the development and manufacturing of advanced ultrasonic transducers and related technologies. Their expertise lies in creating custom solutions for a wide range of applications, including medical diagnostics, non-destructive testing (NDT), industrial process control, and underwater acoustics. The company emphasizes innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction, working closely with clients to design and produce transducers that meet their specific needs.

Their capabilities encompass the entire transducer development process, from initial concept and simulation to prototyping, testing, and manufacturing. They offer a variety of transducer types, including single-element transducers, array transducers, and specialty transducers designed for unique applications. Seattle Ultrasonics also provides services such as transducer repair, characterization, and consulting.

Seattle Ultrasonics serves diverse industries like medical, aerospace, energy, and research institutions. They are committed to providing high-performance ultrasonic solutions that address complex challenges and improve the efficiency and accuracy of various processes. Their website showcases their products, services, and technical expertise, inviting potential customers to contact them for custom solutions and collaborations.
