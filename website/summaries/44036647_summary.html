DDoSecrets publishes 410 GB of heap dumps, hacked from TeleMessage

According to the article, Distributed Denial of Secrets (DDoSecrets) has published a 410 GB archive of heap dumps reportedly hacked from the Telegram messages archive server, Telemessage. The leaked data allegedly contains messages, documents, and other files from 2017 to 2023. The source of the data is unknown, but DDoSecrets has labeled it as obtained via hacking. The data includes messages from both public and private channels.

The article does not offer an analysis of the content of the data, but focuses on the nature of the leak itself.

Key Points:

  • DDoSecrets published a 410 GB archive of heap dumps.
  • The data is reported to be hacked from Telemessage.
  • The data contains messages, documents, and other files from 2017 to 2023.
  • The source of the data is currently unknown.
