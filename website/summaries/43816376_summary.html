National Archives Releases Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena (UAP) Records

The National Archives and Records Administration (NARA) has announced that the agency will be closed on Monday, January 20, 2025, for the federal holiday honoring the birthday of Martin Luther King, Jr. This includes all National Archives research rooms, museums, and Presidential Libraries. All facilities will resume normal operating hours on Tuesday, January 21, 2025.

Key Points:

  • NARA will be closed on January 20, 2025.
  • The closure is for Martin Luther King, Jr. Day.
  • All NARA research rooms, museums, and Presidential Libraries will be closed.
  • Normal operations will resume on January 21, 2025.
