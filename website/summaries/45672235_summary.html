HP SitePrint

Summary

HP's SitePrint is an autonomous robot designed to improve the layout process on construction sites. It automates the task of printing layouts onto the floor, replacing the traditional, manual methods that are often time-consuming, inaccurate, and labor-intensive. SitePrint is designed to be easy to use, offering a user-friendly interface and cloud-based job submission. Users can upload digital layout plans, and the robot autonomously navigates the site, printing the layout with high precision. It can print on various surfaces, including concrete, asphalt, and dirt, and is capable of operating in diverse jobsite conditions. SitePrint is compatible with a variety of surveying total stations, ensuring accurate positioning and integration with existing construction workflows. By automating the layout process, SitePrint aims to significantly reduce the time and cost associated with this task, while also improving accuracy and efficiency. The robot is designed to work alongside construction teams, improving overall project workflows and productivity.
