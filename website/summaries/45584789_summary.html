Google Pixel 10 Pro Fold explodes during JerryRigEverything's durability test

Summary

According to the Dexerto article, a Google Pixel Fold exploded during a durability test conducted by Zack Nelson of the JerryRigEverything YouTube channel. The incident occurred while Nelson was bending the phone. The video shows the device emitting smoke and fire before Nelson quickly throws it to the side. The article notes that while phones occasionally fail during bend tests, they don't typically explode. The Google Pixel Fold is a foldable smartphone released by Google.

Key Points

  • A Google Pixel Fold exploded during a JerryRigEverything durability test.
  • The explosion happened while Zack Nelson was performing a bend test on the phone.
  • The phone emitted smoke and fire.
  • Explosions are not typical outcomes of phone bend tests.
