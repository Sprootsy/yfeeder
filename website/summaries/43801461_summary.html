The primary motivation behind this decision is to protect users from harassment, abuse, and illegal content, which the moderation teams of those problematic instances have been unable or unwilling to address effectively. The author acknowledges the complexity of the situation and the diverse opinions within the community, reiterating their commitment to transparency and user safety. They explain that this decision was made after careful consideration and is intended to create a more positive and inclusive environment for the majority of users on mastodon.online. The post also seeks to dispel misinformation and reassure users that their accounts and data are not at risk due to this action, and that the vast majority of the Fediverse will remain accessible. It acknowledges potential disruptions to some users' interactions with those on the defederated instances, but underscores that the safety and well-being of the mastodon.online community are paramount. Finally, the author encourages users to reach out with questions or concerns and to participate in constructive dialogue about this important issue.
