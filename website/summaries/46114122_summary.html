John Giannandrea to retire from Apple

Summary:

John Giannandrea, Apple's senior vice president of Machine Learning and AI Strategy, will retire from Apple in late 2025. He joined Apple in 2018 and has overseen the company's AI and machine learning efforts, significantly enhancing features and capabilities across Apple's products and services, including advancements in personalization, privacy, and security. Under his leadership, Apple has integrated AI and machine learning into numerous aspects of its ecosystem, improving user experiences and reinforcing Apple's commitment to innovation in these fields. His responsibilities will be transitioned to other leaders within Apple.
