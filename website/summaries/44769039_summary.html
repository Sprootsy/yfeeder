Telo MT1

Summary

Based on the provided URL, this leads to the website for Telo Trucks, an electric truck company. Telo Trucks is introducing a compact electric truck designed to combine the functionality of a full-size pickup with the maneuverability of a smaller vehicle. The Telo truck is designed to fit into a parking space similar to that of a Toyota Corolla while offering the cargo bed length comparable to a Toyota Tacoma.

The company emphasizes the vehicle's innovative design, maximizing interior space and utility while maintaining a small footprint. The Telo truck features a unique midgate design (like the Chevy Avalanche), allowing the rear seats to fold down and the cargo bed to extend into the cabin, significantly increasing cargo-carrying capacity. Furthermore, they aim to produce this electric truck in the USA.

Telo Trucks highlights the truck's expected performance and capabilities. This includes an all-electric powertrain, all-wheel drive, and a targeted range suitable for daily use. It appears they are targeting both individual consumers and commercial customers who need a versatile and eco-friendly vehicle.

Key Points

  • Telo Trucks is an electric truck company.
  • They are developing a compact electric truck with the footprint of a small car but the cargo capacity of a mid-size pickup.
  • The Telo truck utilizes a midgate design to extend the cargo bed into the cabin.
  • The truck is all-electric and all-wheel drive.
  • Telo aims to manufacture their trucks in the USA.
