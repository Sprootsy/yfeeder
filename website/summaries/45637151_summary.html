The White House is already one of the most blocked accounts on Bluesky

According to an article published on TechCrunch on October 19, 2025, the White House's account on Bluesky is already one of the most blocked accounts on the platform. The article highlights the growing trend of users blocking official government accounts and institutions on decentralized social media platforms like Bluesky. While the exact reasons for the high block rate aren't explicitly detailed in this short article, it suggests that users are leveraging the control afforded by these platforms to curate their online experience and potentially limit exposure to official narratives or information sources. The article implies this phenomenon could have implications for how government entities communicate and engage with the public in the evolving social media landscape.

Key points:

  • The White House's Bluesky account is among the most blocked on the platform.
  • Users are increasingly blocking official government accounts on decentralized social media.
  • This trend reflects user control over their online experience.
  • It may impact government communication strategies on social media.
