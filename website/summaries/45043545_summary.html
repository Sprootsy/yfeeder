Firefox Has Moved to Firefox.com

This article is about the Firefox web browser. It highlights Firefox as a browser that puts users first, emphasizing privacy, independence, and innovation. The article promotes the browser's private browsing mode and built-in tracking protection to defend users against intrusive trackers. It celebrates Firefox as an independent alternative to browsers owned by large corporations.

The article also touches upon the various Firefox products and features available across multiple platforms. It encourages users to download Firefox for desktop and mobile, highlighting its availability on Android and iOS. Features mentioned include customizable options, such as themes and extensions, and synchronization across devices.
