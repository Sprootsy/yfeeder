Doing well in your courses: Andrej's advice for success (2013)

The article, written by Andrej Karpathy, provides advice on various aspects of career and personal development, primarily targeted at individuals in technical fields, particularly those interested in artificial intelligence and machine learning. It covers topics ranging from education and skill acquisition to career choices, research, productivity, and mental well-being. The overarching theme is to encourage readers to take initiative, build a strong foundation of knowledge, prioritize learning and execution, and cultivate a resilient and adaptable mindset.

Karpathy emphasizes the importance of a solid education, particularly in mathematics and computer science fundamentals. He suggests focusing on understanding the "why" behind concepts rather than just memorizing formulas. He recommends taking challenging courses and engaging deeply with the material to build a strong theoretical and practical understanding. He also suggests actively working on personal projects to solidify knowledge and gain hands-on experience. Open source projects are specifically highlighted as a way to learn from others, contribute to the community, and build a portfolio.

Regarding career choices, the author encourages readers to consider the long-term trajectory of their careers and to seek out opportunities that offer significant learning and growth potential. He advises against solely chasing prestige or high salaries early in one's career, suggesting instead to prioritize acquiring valuable skills and experience. He suggests to find what one is passionate about and good at, and then align career goals with that intersection. He also emphasizes the value of working with talented and supportive teams. Finding a good mentor is also very important. He underscores the importance of being proactive in seeking out opportunities and networking with professionals in the field.

In terms of research and technical work, the article advocates for a hands-on, empirical approach. He stresses the importance of getting "close to the data" and developing an intuitive understanding of the problem being addressed. He encourages researchers to be creative in their problem-solving and to embrace experimentation. Writing and communication skills are also highlighted as essential for effectively conveying research findings and ideas. Furthermore, the article emphasizes the importance of reproducibility and transparency in research.

The article also addresses the importance of productivity and focus. It recommends strategies for managing time effectively, minimizing distractions, and maintaining a healthy work-life balance. He emphasizes the need to ruthlessly prioritize tasks and focus on the most impactful activities. The author also touches on the importance of physical and mental well-being, advising readers to prioritize sleep, exercise, and mindfulness practices to maintain optimal performance and prevent burnout.

Finally, the article encourages readers to cultivate a growth mindset and to embrace challenges as opportunities for learning. He stresses the importance of resilience in the face of setbacks and the ability to adapt to change. The author also suggests that it's crucial to be curious, to stay up-to-date with the latest advancements in the field, and to continuously seek out new knowledge and skills. He also advocates for being critical of one's own assumptions and beliefs and continuously seeking feedback from others.

Key Points:

  • Education & Fundamentals: Build a strong foundation in math and computer science, focusing on understanding "why" rather than just memorization.
  • Practical Experience: Work on personal projects and contribute to open source to gain hands-on experience.
  • Career Choices: Prioritize learning and growth over prestige or salary early in your career. Find opportunities that align with your passions and strengths.
  • Research Approach: Embrace a hands-on, empirical approach to research. Get close to the data and develop an intuitive understanding of the problem.
  • Productivity & Focus: Manage time effectively, minimize distractions, and prioritize tasks ruthlessly.
  • Well-being: Prioritize sleep, exercise, and mindfulness for optimal performance and to prevent burnout.
  • Growth Mindset: Embrace challenges, cultivate resilience, and continuously seek new knowledge and skills.
