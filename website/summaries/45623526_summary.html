Jeep wrangler owners waiting for answers week after an update bricked their cars

Jeep Wrangler 4xe owners are experiencing issues after a recent over-the-air (OTA) software update bricked their vehicles. Owners report that after the update, their Wranglers are completely non-functional, unable to start or shift out of park. The exact cause of the problem is unknown, but it appears widespread, affecting a significant number of 4xe vehicles. The vehicles are rendered useless, leaving owners stranded. Stellantis, Jeep's parent company, has acknowledged the issue and is working on a solution, but a fix has not yet been released. Owners are frustrated with the lack of communication and the extended downtime, as they rely on their vehicles for daily transportation. Many owners are reporting this is happening during vacations.

Key points:

  • A recent OTA software update is bricking Jeep Wrangler 4xe vehicles.
  • Affected vehicles are rendered non-functional.
  • Stellantis is aware of the problem and working on a fix.
  • Owners are frustrated by the lack of communication and the delay in resolving the issue.
