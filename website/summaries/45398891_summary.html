NixOS moderation team resigns over NixOS Steering Committee's interference

The article is a statement from members of the NixOS Discourse moderation team. It addresses the community regarding moderation policies, transparency, and the handling of conflicts within the community.

The moderation team acknowledges shortcomings in their enforcement of the Code of Conduct, particularly concerning issues related to controversial topics such as the war in Ukraine. They recognize that their actions and inactions have caused harm and division within the community. The team expresses regret for not consistently upholding the standards of respectful and constructive communication outlined in the Code of Conduct.

The statement emphasizes the importance of creating a welcoming and inclusive environment for all members of the NixOS community. It reiterates the commitment to fostering respectful dialogue and preventing harassment or discrimination. The moderation team commits to improving its processes and communication to ensure fair and consistent enforcement of the Code of Conduct.

Specifically, the statement addresses concerns about the use of moderation tools and the potential for bias in their application. The team acknowledges the need for greater transparency in moderation decisions and commits to providing clearer explanations for actions taken. They also recognize the importance of addressing systemic issues that contribute to conflict and division within the community.

Furthermore, the moderation team invites community feedback on their policies and practices. They express a willingness to engage in open dialogue with community members to identify areas for improvement and to collaboratively develop solutions to address challenges. The team emphasizes that effective moderation requires a shared commitment from all members of the community to uphold the principles of respectful communication and inclusivity.

The statement concludes by reaffirming the moderation team's dedication to serving the NixOS community and fostering a positive and productive environment for collaboration and innovation. They acknowledge that building trust and rebuilding relationships will require ongoing effort and a willingness to learn from past mistakes. The team expresses hope that through open communication, collaboration, and a shared commitment to the Code of Conduct, the NixOS community can overcome its challenges and continue to thrive.
