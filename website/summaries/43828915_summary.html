Oracle engineers caused five days software outage at U.S. hospitals

Summary

According to a report by the Wall Street Journal, a multi-day software outage at U.S. hospitals in April 2024 was caused by Oracle engineers. The outage affected hospitals across the country, disrupting patient care and causing significant operational challenges. The issues stemmed from a software update that was rolled out by Oracle, leading to widespread system failures.

Key Points

  • A software outage impacted U.S. hospitals in April 2024.
  • The outage lasted for multiple days.
  • Oracle engineers were responsible for the software update that caused the problem.
  • The outage disrupted patient care and hospital operations.
  • The report was published by the Wall Street Journal.
