Browser extension and local backend that automatically archives YouTube videos

Starchive is a self-hosted, cloud-based solution for storing and managing files. It is an open-source alternative to services like Google Drive or Dropbox, offering users complete control over their data and storage infrastructure. Written in Rust, Starchive prioritizes performance and security. It allows users to upload files, organize them into folders, and manage permissions. Starchive can be hosted on various platforms, providing flexibility for users with different technical skills and infrastructure preferences.
