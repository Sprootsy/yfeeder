Waymo has received our pilot permit allowing for commercial operations at SFO

Summary:

Waymo has received a Drivered Pilot permit from the California Department of Motor Vehicles (DMV) to operate its autonomous ride-hailing service in San Francisco (SFO). This permit allows Waymo to charge passengers for rides in its Waymo One vehicles, with a safety driver present. The service area includes the entire city of San Francisco, extending to parts of Daly City, South San Francisco, Colma, and Brisbane.

Key Points:

  • Waymo received a Drivered Pilot permit from the California DMV.
  • The permit allows Waymo to operate a ride-hailing service with safety drivers in San Francisco.
  • Waymo can now charge passengers for rides.
  • The service area includes San Francisco and parts of Daly City, South San Francisco, Colma, and Brisbane.
