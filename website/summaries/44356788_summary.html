Fairphone 6 is switching to a new design that's even more sustainable

The article discusses leaked renders of the upcoming Fairphone 6, highlighting its sustainable and modular design. The Fairphone 6 is expected to continue the company's commitment to using recycled materials and ethical sourcing, aligning with its focus on environmental responsibility. The renders suggest a focus on user-friendly repairs and upgrades, allowing users to replace components easily. The article emphasizes Fairphone's unique position in the smartphone market as a company prioritizing sustainability and ethical practices over simply competing on specifications or features.
