Investigation into 4chan and its compliance with duties to protect its users

Summary of Ofcom's Investigation into 4chan

Ofcom, the UK's communications regulator, conducted an investigation into 4chan's compliance with duties to protect its users from illegal content, specifically focusing on the requirements outlined in the Online Safety Act. The investigation examined 4chan's systems and processes for identifying and removing illegal content, as well as its measures to prevent users from encountering such material. Ofcom scrutinized 4chan's content moderation policies, reporting mechanisms, and overall approach to online safety to determine whether they meet the standards expected under UK law.

Key Points

  • Ofcom investigated 4chan's compliance with the Online Safety Act.
  • The investigation focused on illegal content.
  • Ofcom examined 4chan's systems for identifying and removing illegal content.
  • Ofcom scrutinized 4chan's content moderation policies and reporting mechanisms.
