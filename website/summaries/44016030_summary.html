Lufthansa plane flown by autopilot after pilot faints in cockpit

A Lufthansa Airbus A320 flying from Bilbao, Spain, to Munich, Germany, had to be flown mostly by autopilot after the pilot lost consciousness mid-flight. The co-pilot took over, declared an emergency, and managed the situation with the assistance of air traffic control. The plane landed safely in Munich. No passengers were harmed.
