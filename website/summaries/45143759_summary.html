US special forces killed North Korean civilians in botched 2019 mission

According to a New York Times report, a U.S. special operations team was involved in a botched mission in North Korea in 2019 that resulted in the deaths of several North Korean civilians. The alleged operation involved a raid on a North Korean building. The New York Times cited current and former administration officials familiar with the events.

Key Points:

  • U.S. special forces allegedly killed North Korean civilians during a 2019 mission.
  • The mission was described as botched.
  • The New York Times cited current and former administration officials as sources.
