Air Force unit suspends use of Sig Sauer pistol after shooting death of airman

According to an article published by NHPR on July 23, 2025, the New Hampshire Attorney General's office released a report regarding the shooting death of a U.S. Air Force airman. The airman died by suicide at the Sig Sauer Academy in Epping, New Hampshire, in 2023.

The Attorney General's office determined that the airman died from a self-inflicted gunshot wound to the head, using a Sig Sauer P320 pistol. The report concluded that the death was a suicide and that no criminal charges would be filed.

The article does not provide details about the airman's identity. It specifies that the incident took place at the Sig Sauer Academy but does not clarify the airman's affiliation with the academy, if any.

The report offers no additional information surrounding the circumstances of the airman's death, or a potential motive.

  • A U.S. Air Force airman died by suicide at the Sig Sauer Academy in Epping, New Hampshire, in 2023.
  • The airman used a Sig Sauer P320 pistol.

  • No criminal charges will be filed.
