Apple reports fourth quarter results

Apple's press release, dated October 30, 2025, announces the company's financial results for the fourth quarter of 2025. Apple reported a quarterly revenue of $119.6 billion, which represents a 9 percent increase year over year. The company also announced quarterly earnings per diluted share of $1.86, marking a 14 percent increase compared to the previous year.

Tim Cook, Apple’s CEO, highlights the record fourth-quarter performance, driven by strong demand for the new iPhone 17 lineup and continued growth in the services sector. He also mentions the company's commitment to innovation and its focus on developing groundbreaking technologies.

Luca Maestri, Apple’s CFO, expresses satisfaction with the company's financial results, noting the robust growth across all product categories and geographic segments. He emphasizes the strong operating cash flow, which allows Apple to continue investing in its long-term growth initiatives and return capital to shareholders. Apple's board of directors has declared a cash dividend of $0.28 per share of the company’s common stock, payable on November 15, 2025, to shareholders of record as of November 12, 2025.

The press release also includes Apple’s guidance for the first fiscal quarter of 2026, anticipating revenue between $128 billion and $132 billion. It provides estimates for gross margin, operating expenses, other income/(expense), and the tax rate.

The announcement concludes with information about Apple's conference call scheduled for October 30, 2025, to discuss the financial results further. It also provides standard legal disclaimers regarding forward-looking statements, which are subject to risks and uncertainties that could cause actual results to differ materially from those projected.

Key points:

  • Apple's Q4 2025 revenue reached $119.6 billion, a 9% year-over-year increase.
  • Earnings per diluted share were $1.86, a 14% increase year-over-year.
  • Strong iPhone 17 demand and Services growth drove the record quarter.
  • Apple declared a cash dividend of $0.28 per share.
  • Q1 2026 revenue is projected to be between $128 billion and $132 billion.
