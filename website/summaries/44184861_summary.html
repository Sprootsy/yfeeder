Ada and SPARK enter the automotive ISO-26262 market with Nvidia

Summary

AdaCore announced a collaboration with NVIDIA to bring its Ada and SPARK technologies to the automotive market, specifically targeting ISO 26262 compliance on the NVIDIA DRIVE Orin platform. This collaboration enables automotive manufacturers and suppliers to develop safety-critical applications with a high level of assurance, leveraging AdaCore's expertise in formal methods and high-integrity software development. By using the Ada and SPARK toolset, developers can create more reliable and secure automotive systems, reducing the risk of software-related failures. The integration with the NVIDIA DRIVE Orin platform offers a powerful combination of hardware and software technologies for advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) and autonomous driving applications.

Key Points

  • AdaCore is collaborating with NVIDIA to bring Ada and SPARK technologies to the automotive market.
  • The collaboration targets ISO 26262 compliance on the NVIDIA DRIVE Orin platform.
  • Ada and SPARK are designed for developing safety-critical applications with high assurance.
  • The toolset helps create more reliable and secure automotive systems, reducing software-related failures.
  • The integration with NVIDIA DRIVE Orin supports advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) and autonomous driving applications.
