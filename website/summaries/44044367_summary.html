Google AI Ultra

The article announces the Google One AI Premium plan, which includes access to Google's most powerful AI model, Gemini Ultra 1.0, and other AI-powered features. This new plan provides users with 2TB of storage and access to Gemini Ultra through Gemini Advanced. Gemini Advanced allows for more complex reasoning, following instructions, coding, and creative collaboration. Initially, Gemini Advanced is available in English, with more languages to be added. Subscribers can use Gemini Advanced within the Gemini web application. Google is also integrating Gemini into other applications, such as Google Docs and Slides, to assist with writing, brainstorming, summarizing, and creating templates. These features will be previewed in Labs. The AI Premium plan also includes all the benefits of the existing Google One Premium plan, such as increased storage, Google Workspace premium features, and more. The Google One AI Premium plan costs $22.99 per month. For a limited time, new users can get a free trial for two months. The article emphasizes the plan's focus on providing advanced AI capabilities and improving user productivity across various Google services.

Key Points:

  • Google introduces the Google One AI Premium plan.
  • The plan includes access to Gemini Ultra 1.0 via Gemini Advanced.
  • Gemini Advanced offers improved reasoning, instruction following, coding, and creative collaboration capabilities.
  • Gemini Advanced is initially available in English, with more languages planned.
  • Gemini is being integrated into Google Docs and Slides for writing and brainstorming assistance (preview in Labs).
  • The AI Premium plan includes 2TB of storage and all existing Google One Premium benefits.
  • The plan costs $22.99 per month, with a limited-time offer of a two-month free trial for new users.
