US Dept of Interior denies canceling largest solar project after axing review

Summary

The Department of the Interior has canceled its review of the proposed Gemini Solar Project in Nevada, which was slated to be one of the largest solar installations in the U.S. The project, initially approved under the Trump administration, faced ongoing scrutiny and legal challenges. The cancellation halts further progress on the project's development.

Key Points

  • The Department of the Interior canceled the review of the Gemini Solar Project in Nevada.
  • The project was one of the largest proposed solar installations in the United States.
  • The project was initially approved under the Trump administration.
  • The cancellation halts further development of the project.
