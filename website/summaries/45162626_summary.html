Intel Arc Pro B50 GPU Launched at $349 for Compact Workstations

Summary:

The Intel Arc Pro B50 GPU has been launched, targeting compact workstations. This single-slot graphics card is designed for professional use, focusing on efficiency and reliability in a small form factor. The card features 8GB of GDDR6 memory and is based on the ACM-G11 GPU, offering a balance of performance and power consumption for tasks like CAD, content creation, and data visualization. The Arc Pro B50 supports modern features such as ray tracing and AI acceleration, aligning it with current industry standards for professional applications. It aims to provide a cost-effective solution for professionals who need a compact yet capable workstation GPU. The card is supported by Intel's professional drivers and certifications, ensuring compatibility and stability with various professional software suites. Its compact design makes it suitable for small form factor workstations where space is limited but performance is still critical.

Key Points:

  • Intel Arc Pro B50 GPU launched for compact workstations.
  • Single-slot design for small form factor systems.
  • 8GB GDDR6 memory.
  • Based on ACM-G11 GPU.
  • Designed for professional applications like CAD and content creation.
  • Supports ray tracing and AI acceleration.
  • Supported by Intel's professional drivers and certifications.
