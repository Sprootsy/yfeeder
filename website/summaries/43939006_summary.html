Man 'Disappeared' by ICE Was on El Salvador Flight Manifest, Hacked Data Shows

According to a report by 404 Media, a man who disappeared after traveling to El Salvador to meet a woman he met online, was listed on the flight manifest obtained in the hack of the airline ICE, which is owned by Avianca. The man, identified as Josh, traveled to El Salvador on February 29, 2024. He told his family that he had met a woman online and intended to meet her in person. After arriving in El Salvador, Josh ceased communication with his family. His family became concerned and began to investigate his disappearance. They contacted the authorities in El Salvador, but were not able to obtain any information about his whereabouts. The family then contacted 404 Media, who were able to confirm that Josh was listed on the ICE flight manifest, confirming that he did fly to El Salvador as he had said. His current whereabouts and status remain unknown. 404 Media gained access to the flight manifest through a hack of the ICE airline's systems. The manifest contained the names, flight numbers, and other personal information of passengers who flew on ICE flights.

  • He was going to meet a woman he met online.
  • Josh is listed on the flight manifest obtained in the hack of the airline ICE.

  • 404 Media confirmed his presence on the flight manifest.
  • The flight manifest was obtained through a hack of ICE's systems.
