Xfinity using WiFi signals in your house to detect motion

Summary:

The Xfinity article discusses the WiFi Motion feature available to Xfinity Internet subscribers with compatible xFi Gateways. WiFi Motion utilizes the existing WiFi network to sense motion within the home without requiring additional hardware like cameras or sensors. It works by analyzing disruptions in WiFi signals caused by moving objects or people. When motion is detected, the system can send notifications through the Xfinity app.

To use WiFi Motion, users must have a compatible xFi Gateway (XB3, XB6, XB7, and XB8 models are supported) and the Xfinity app. The feature needs to be activated through the app, and users can then customize settings such as motion sensitivity and notification preferences. The article provides step-by-step instructions on how to activate, configure, and use WiFi Motion.

The article also outlines important considerations, including limitations such as potential false alarms due to environmental factors or pets, and the feature's dependence on a stable WiFi connection. Optimal performance is achieved when the xFi Gateway is centrally located and elevated. Furthermore, it mentions that WiFi Motion is not a substitute for a professional security system and should be used as a complementary tool for home awareness.

Troubleshooting tips are provided for common issues, such as frequent false alerts. These suggestions include adjusting the motion sensitivity settings and ensuring the environment is free of factors that may cause signal interference. The article emphasizes that the effectiveness of WiFi Motion can vary depending on the home's layout, building materials, and other factors that can impact WiFi signal propagation.

Finally, the article details how to disable the WiFi Motion feature if desired. This can be done through the Xfinity app, effectively turning off motion detection and associated notifications.

Key Points:

  • WiFi Motion uses existing WiFi signals to detect movement within a home.
  • Requires a compatible xFi Gateway (XB3, XB6, XB7, or XB8) and the Xfinity app.
  • Activation and configuration are done through the Xfinity app.
  • Users can customize motion sensitivity and notification settings.
  • Performance can be affected by home layout, building materials, and environmental factors.
  • Not a replacement for a professional security system.
  • Provides troubleshooting tips for false alarms and other issues.
  • Can be disabled through the Xfinity app.
