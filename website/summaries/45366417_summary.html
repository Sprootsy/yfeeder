Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024 is coming to Playstation 5 on December 8th

Summary:

The article announces that Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024 will be available on PlayStation 5. This marks the first time the simulator will be available on a PlayStation platform. The announcement was made during the Xbox Games Showcase 2024. The game will be released on November 19th, 2024.

Key Points:

  • Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024 is coming to PlayStation 5.
  • This is the first time the simulator will be available on PlayStation.
  • The release date is November 19th, 2024.
  • The announcement was made at the Xbox Games Showcase 2024.
