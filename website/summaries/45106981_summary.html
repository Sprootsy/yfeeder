Vijaye Raji to become CTO of Applications with acquisition of Statsig

Summary:

OpenAI has announced that Vijaye Raji will become the Chief Technology Officer (CTO) of Applications, following OpenAI's acquisition of Statsig. Raji was the founder and CEO of Statsig, a company focused on feature management and experimentation. The acquisition aims to enhance OpenAI's ability to build and scale applications leveraging AI. Raji brings extensive experience in building developer tools and platforms, having previously worked at Facebook where he led the development of developer tools.

Key Points:

  • Vijaye Raji, formerly CEO of Statsig, will become CTO of Applications at OpenAI.
  • OpenAI has acquired Statsig, a feature management and experimentation platform.
  • The acquisition is intended to bolster OpenAI's application development capabilities.
  • Raji has a background in building developer tools, including experience at Facebook.
