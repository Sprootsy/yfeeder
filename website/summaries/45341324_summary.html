Fall Foliage Map 2025

This article is about a Fall Foliage Prediction Map provided by Explore Fall, designed to help people plan trips to see the changing colors of leaves across the United States. The map provides estimated dates for when different regions will experience peak fall foliage, ranging from "No Change" to "Past Peak". Users can interact with the map to see the foliage forecast for specific dates and regions, aiding in planning leaf-peeping excursions. The accuracy of the predictions can be affected by weather conditions.

Key Points:

  • Explore Fall provides a Fall Foliage Prediction Map.
  • The map forecasts the progression of fall foliage across the United States.
  • The map uses a color-coded system to indicate the stage of foliage change.
  • The stages range from "No Change" to "Past Peak".
  • Users can select dates to see the predicted foliage status for that time.
  • The map is intended to help people plan trips to view fall colors.
  • Weather conditions can impact the accuracy of the foliage predictions.
