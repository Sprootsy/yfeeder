France threatens GrapheneOS with arrests / server seizure for refusing backdoors

The article is a statement from La Quadrature du Net regarding the Digital Services Act (DSA) and its potential impact on freedom of expression and the fight against illegal content. They express concerns that the DSA, while aiming to regulate online platforms, could lead to increased censorship and surveillance if not implemented carefully. La Quadrature du Net highlights the risk of platforms over-complying with content removal requests to avoid fines, potentially silencing legitimate voices and limiting access to information. The statement emphasizes the importance of protecting users' rights to freedom of expression and privacy while addressing harmful content online. They advocate for safeguards to prevent abuse of content moderation systems and to ensure transparency and accountability in the enforcement of the DSA. La Quadrature du Net stresses the need for a balanced approach that effectively tackles illegal content without undermining fundamental rights and freedoms.
