“Learning how to Learn” will be next generation's most needed skill

The article reports on a Google AI scientist who claims that Google's Gemini AI model has demonstrated general-purpose next-token prediction, suggesting it has acquired a world model and possesses genuine generalization skill. The scientist suggests that Gemini's ability to perform well on tasks it wasn't specifically trained for indicates a significant step towards artificial general intelligence (AGI). The scientist posted on a social media site that, in his opinion, Gemini had attained a level of generalisation that was very promising for the future of AI development.

Key Points:

  • Google AI scientist claims Gemini has achieved general-purpose next-token prediction.
  • The scientist believes Gemini has acquired a world model.
  • The scientist posits that Gemini demonstrates genuine generalization skill.
  • The scientist suggests this indicates a step towards AGI.
