America is in a serious jobs slump

Summary:

According to a CNN article published on September 3, 2025, the U.S. job market showed signs of cooling in July, as indicated by the latest Job Openings and Labor Turnover Survey (JOLTS) data. Job openings decreased, and layoffs remained low, suggesting a moderation in labor demand. The report highlights a shift in the labor market dynamics, with potential implications for future economic growth and inflation.

Key Points:

  • Job openings decreased in July.
  • Layoffs remained low.
  • The JOLTS data suggests a cooling in the U.S. labor market.
