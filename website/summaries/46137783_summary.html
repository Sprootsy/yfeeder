Micron Announces Exit from Crucial Consumer Business

Summary:

Micron Technology, Inc. has announced its decision to exit the Crucial consumer storage business. This strategic move will allow Micron to increase its focus on higher-margin opportunities within the data center, cloud, and client sectors. The company expects to complete the exit during fiscal year 2025. Micron plans to increase focus and investments in other parts of the business like data center, cloud and client.

Key Points:

  • Micron is exiting the Crucial consumer storage business.
  • The exit is expected to be completed during fiscal year 2025.
  • This move is intended to increase focus on data center, cloud, and client sectors.
