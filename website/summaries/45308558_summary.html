Disney+ cancellation page crashes as customers rush to quit

According to the Yahoo! Creators article, Disney's customer service webpage experienced technical difficulties as users attempted to cancel their subscriptions. This surge in cancellation attempts appears to be related to Disney's suspension of Jimmy Kimmel following his interview with former President Donald Trump. The article suggests that the suspension has angered some viewers, leading them to take action by canceling their Disney subscriptions.

Key Points:

  • Disney's customer service page for cancellations reportedly experienced technical difficulties.
  • The technical difficulties occurred amidst a rush of customers attempting to cancel their subscriptions.
  • The surge in cancellations is linked to Disney's decision to suspend Jimmy Kimmel.
  • The suspension followed an interview with Donald Trump that sparked controversy.
  • Some viewers are expressing their dissatisfaction with Disney's action by canceling their subscriptions.
