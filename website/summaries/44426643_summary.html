Apple weighs using Anthropic or OpenAI to power Siri

According to a Bloomberg report, Apple is considering replacing its current Siri AI with technology from either Anthropic (Claude) or OpenAI (ChatGPT). Apple is reportedly in discussions with both companies. The aim is to enhance Siri's capabilities with more advanced AI large language models (LLMs). The current iteration of Siri has been criticized for being less advanced than competing AI assistants. By integrating technology from Anthropic or OpenAI, Apple hopes to offer a more robust and competitive AI experience to its users. The specific nature of the integration and the timeline for any potential changes remain unclear, but the discussions indicate a strategic shift towards leveraging external expertise to bolster Apple's AI offerings.

Key Points:

  • Apple is exploring replacing Siri's AI with LLMs from Anthropic (Claude) or OpenAI (ChatGPT).
  • The move aims to improve Siri's capabilities and make it more competitive with other AI assistants.
  • Apple is in discussions with both Anthropic and OpenAI.
  • The details and timeline of potential integration are not yet known.
  • The discussions signify a strategic move to enhance Apple's AI capabilities using external expertise.
