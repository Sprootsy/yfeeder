Charlie Kirk killed at event in Utah

Summary: According to the NBC News article, a shooting occurred outside a Charlie Kirk event at the Utah County Convention Center in Provo. The incident took place around 6 p.m. local time, amidst a crowd protesting Kirk's appearance. Police reported that a physical altercation preceded the shooting. Two individuals were detained, and one person was shot. The victim was transported to the hospital. The convention center was temporarily placed on lockdown.

Key Points:

  • Shooting occurred outside a Charlie Kirk event in Provo, Utah.
  • The shooting occurred amidst a protest.
  • A physical altercation preceded the shooting.
  • One person was shot and transported to the hospital.
  • Two people were detained.
  • The Utah County Convention Center was temporarily placed on lockdown.
