Melbourne man discovers extensive model train network underneath house

Summary

A Melbourne man made an unexpected discovery after purchasing a new house: approximately $50,000 in cash hidden inside. The man, known only as "John," found the money while doing renovations on the property. The cash was concealed in an unusual location within the house. John was surprised by the discovery and unsure of what to do with the money. He contacted the police to report his finding. The police took possession of the money and initiated an investigation to determine its origin and whether it was linked to any criminal activity. The police thoroughly searched the house for any further hidden items or clues. After a period of time, the police were unable to determine the rightful owner of the money or connect it to any crime. As a result, and according to Australian law regarding found property, the money was returned to John. John expressed his surprise and good fortune at being able to keep the money.
