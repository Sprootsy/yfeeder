How Riot Games is fighting the war against video game hackers

Summary

According to the article, Riot Games is heavily invested in combating video game hackers to maintain fair play and protect its games, particularly Valorant. The company employs a multi-faceted approach encompassing technical innovation, legal action, and community engagement. Riot has developed its own anti-cheat system, Vanguard, which operates at a kernel level to detect and prevent cheating attempts. While controversial due to its deep system access, Vanguard has proven effective in reducing cheating in Valorant. Riot acknowledges the ongoing arms race between anti-cheat developers and hackers, emphasizing the need for continuous improvement and adaptation of its anti-cheat measures.

Beyond technical solutions, Riot Games actively pursues legal action against cheat developers and sellers, aiming to disrupt the cheat ecosystem and deter future malicious actors. These lawsuits can involve significant financial penalties and reputational damage for those involved in creating and distributing cheats.

Riot recognizes the importance of community involvement in the fight against cheating. The company encourages players to report suspicious behavior and provides channels for communication and feedback. By fostering a sense of shared responsibility, Riot aims to create a gaming environment where cheating is not tolerated and where players actively contribute to maintaining fair play. Riot Games emphasizes that combating cheating is a continuous effort, requiring ongoing investment, innovation, and collaboration with the community.

Key Points

  • Riot Games is actively fighting video game hackers to ensure fair play, particularly in Valorant.
  • Riot uses Vanguard, a kernel-level anti-cheat system, to detect and prevent cheating in Valorant.
  • Riot Games is aware of the constant arms race between anti-cheat systems and cheat developers, requiring continuous improvement.
  • Riot takes legal action against cheat developers and sellers to disrupt the cheat ecosystem.
  • Riot Games encourages community involvement in reporting suspicious behavior and maintaining fair play.
  • Combating cheating is an ongoing and evolving effort that requires continuous investment.
