ChatGPT terms disallow its use in providing legal and medical advice to others

OpenAI has updated its policies to prevent ChatGPT from providing medical or legal advice. The company has explicitly prohibited its AI chatbot from offering opinions on topics that require professional expertise, such as medical conditions, legal matters, or financial advice. This change is part of OpenAI's effort to ensure its AI tools are used responsibly and do not provide inaccurate or harmful information in sensitive areas. The updated policy reflects a growing awareness of the potential risks associated with using AI for advice that should come from qualified professionals. OpenAI is actively working to refine its AI models to better understand and adhere to these guidelines, aiming to create a safer and more reliable user experience.
