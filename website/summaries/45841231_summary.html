Europeans recognize Zohran Mamdani's policies as 'normal'

According to the article, if Donald Trump wins the 2024 presidential election, a network of conservative organizations is prepared to implement a detailed plan to reshape the US government and shift it further to the right. This plan, known as Project 2025, involves policy changes, executive orders, and legislative proposals across various areas, including the economy, social issues, and foreign policy. The aim is to dismantle parts of the federal government, centralize power in the presidency, and promote a conservative Christian worldview. Key personnel appointments are also a crucial aspect of this project, with the intention of filling government positions with individuals aligned with these goals.

Zohran Kwame Mamdani is mentioned as someone whose policies, once considered radical, are becoming increasingly mainstream in Europe. The article suggests a parallel between the potential normalization of far-right policies in the US through Project 2025 and the acceptance of formerly fringe ideas in Europe.

The article emphasizes the scope and coordination of Project 2025, portraying it as a significant and well-funded effort to fundamentally alter the direction of the United States. It raises concerns about the potential consequences of implementing such a plan, particularly regarding democratic norms, civil liberties, and the role of government.
