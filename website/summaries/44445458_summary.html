Sony's Mark Cerny Has Worked on "Big Chunks of RDNA 5" with AMD

According to a recent Moore's Law is Dead report, Mark Cerny, the lead architect of the PlayStation consoles, has been heavily involved with AMD in the development of their RDNA 5 architecture. The report suggests Cerny has contributed to significant portions of RDNA 5. While the exact nature and extent of his involvement remain unclear, it implies a continued collaboration between Sony and AMD beyond the existing partnership that produced the custom RDNA 2-based GPU for the PlayStation 5.

Key Points:

  • Mark Cerny, PlayStation's lead architect, is reportedly working with AMD on RDNA 5.
  • Cerny's involvement extends to "big chunks" of the RDNA 5 architecture.
  • This collaboration suggests a continued partnership between Sony and AMD beyond the PS5's RDNA 2 GPU.
