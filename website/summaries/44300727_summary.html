Tesla Robotaxi launch is a dangerous game of smoke and mirrors

Summary:

The article discusses the upcoming launch of Tesla's Robotaxi service, characterizing it as a potentially "dangerous game" filled with "smoke and mirrors." It suggests that Tesla's claims of full self-driving capabilities are overstated and that the technology is not yet ready for widespread deployment without significant human oversight. The author raises concerns about the safety and reliability of the Robotaxi system, pointing to Tesla's history of overpromising and underdelivering on autonomous driving features. The article also highlights the regulatory hurdles Tesla faces in obtaining approval for its Robotaxi service and questions whether the company can meet the necessary safety standards. It suggests that the Robotaxi launch may be more about boosting Tesla's stock price and attracting investors than about genuinely revolutionizing transportation. Ultimately, the author expresses skepticism about Tesla's ability to deliver a safe and fully autonomous Robotaxi service in the near future.
